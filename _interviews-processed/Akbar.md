## The Before State
Before Titan, Drayton Prime used ProScript Connect. Staff were printing scripts, printing labels, checking, packaging — four separate manual steps. Owings would pile up to 200 at the end of the week. The team was staying at the pharmacy until 2am to clear work. One staff member told Akbar: "This is hell." Akbar could not step back from day-to-day operations without everything falling apart. "You have to have eyes at the back of your head."

## The Tipping Point
A staff member who had been staying late regularly, one month after Titan was implemented, turned to Akbar and said: "We can go home on time." That moment crystallised what had changed.

## What Changed
Titan streamlined all four manual steps into one connected workflow. The prescription location feature was transformational — finding any prescription in under 30 seconds versus hunting through 200 bags. Titan AI initially met staff resistance but converted them after one pharmacist used it after a gap and said "my day's been so much easier." Akbar grew items from 16,000 to 32,000 per month after implementing Titan — "it springs up almost instantaneously."

## The After State
Drayton Prime now does 32,000 items per month (~2,000 items/day), finishing all work by end of day with typically only 2 Owings by Friday. Akbar no longer needs to be present day-to-day — the pharmacy runs whether he is there or not. They are listed as doing the most Pharmacy First in their area and ~350 NMS per month. Akbar saved the equivalent of 3 full-time staff members. He can now focus on "strategic moves" rather than daily operations.

## Best Direct Quotes
- "Without Titan, I don't think I'd be able to step away. I think I'd have to be a lot more hands on."
- "We grew in items quite doubled, basically. So from 16 to 32 — that's quite big change."
- "Before Titan, you'd be there till two o'clock in the morning. And I had a staff member who turned around looked at me and said, 'This is hell.' And then a month after we got Titan, they looked at me and said, 'We can go home on time.'"
- "Titan gives you the confidence to be able to do that — you can always look back on the system and get on the phone and speak to people about X, Y and Z, but you don't have to be there day to day."
- "I don't want anyone to get Titan. Stay paper based, and stay in your zone, because we can take over, and we're happy to take over."
- "I don't need a 300,000 pound robot. I've got Titan. It does the job for me per day."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Akbar's focus is growing services — especially Pharmacy First and private services. He wants to continue stepping further back from day-to-day operations and position Drayton Prime as the destination pharmacy for the area. He sees Titan as the enabler that gives him the freedom to think strategically.
