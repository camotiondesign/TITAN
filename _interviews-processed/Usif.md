## The Before State
BNP's team was on ProScript "through and through" for 10–15 years. Lauren describes the transition as "quite hard to begin with" as expected. The 25,000-item site was described as "the biggest challenge" with a robot on site that had implementation issues on day one.

## The Tipping Point
Not a structured before/after case study — this is an on-site go-live testimonial. The robot had issues on day one but this turned out to be an advantage: it gave the staff time to learn Titan manually first.

## What Changed
Despite robot issues on day one, the team adapted and used Titan manually. The structured staff support from the implementation team was highlighted by both Usif and Lauren as the key differentiator. "Taking the time with the staff is the main thing."

## The After State
Go-live was deemed successful. "Staff have taken well to it. They're understanding slowly the process." Lauren says going forward it will "be great." Usif notes the 7th site done with 5 more to go.

## Best Direct Quotes
- "This is the biggest site yet — 25,000 items has been our biggest challenge. But the staff have taken well to it."
- "We had a little bit of trouble with robot implementation on day one, but it allowed everyone a bit of time to use Titan to get used to it, doing it manually."
- "The team has been fantastic throughout this whole process — taking the time with the staff is the main thing."
- "I've been ProScript through and through for the last 10–15 years. The transition was — has to be expected — quite hard to begin with, but everybody took it on board, and I think going forward it would be great."
- "As an ICT, what benefits do you see for you? I think it frees up a lot of time — obviously the accuracy is there as well."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
BNP Healthcare continues rolling out Titan across all remaining sites (5 more at time of recording). The group's broader vision (per Yusuf's full interview) is to centralize dispensing, free pharmacists for consultation, and grow services significantly.
