## The Before State
Kanav didn't switch to Titan — he started with it from day one of owning a brand new pharmacy. He saw other pharmacies spending all day claiming prescriptions manually and getting behind on dispensing. His previous workplace (before owning) was an example of this dysfunction.

## The Tipping Point
"The last pharmacy I worked at used to claim prescriptions all day long and get behind dispensing. But I don't do claiming here." Titan's one-click claim-all function was a revelation when Kanav first understood it as a new owner.

## What Changed
Titan's one-click claiming freed Kanav from the admin trap that bogs down other pharmacies. His dispensing team handles all dispensing independently — Kanav only does clinical checks. His time goes almost entirely into the consultation room: earwax removal, travel health, weight loss, all other services. He is "pretty relaxed" in an interview filmed during a busy period at the pharmacy.

## The After State
Kanav has built a pharmacy known in the community for service efficiency — "really good response from the community saying your services are really efficient." Multiple services running simultaneously, bags ready when patients arrive, everything flowing through Titan's workflow. Word of mouth is growing the practice from day one.

## Best Direct Quotes
- "We only do it because of Titan. Since day one, it's a brand new shop. It's a brand new business for me. Since day one, I've always had Titan, and it makes me do a lot of other services and generate revenue on that."
- "I have my personal time. I have time to do all a lot of other things and a lot of other services, which we offer in our store."
- "The last pharmacy I worked at used to claim prescriptions all day long and get behind dispensing. But I don't do claiming here."
- "Through Titan, all you do is you click on one claim, and it claims all the prescriptions for the day."
- "As a brand new business, I've had really good response from the community saying your services are really efficient — and that's purely because of Titan."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Continue growing word-of-mouth referrals. Scale services further as the pharmacy grows. Community efficiency reputation is the growth engine.
