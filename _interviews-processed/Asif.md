## Quick Stats
- Items per month (before): ~8,000
- Items per month (after): 14,500–15,000
- Hours saved: not stated precisely; described as "so much better"
- Staff: 5 part-time, 2 full-time, 1 Saturday staff = ~11 FTE total; 2 pharmacists; previously had ACT (checking technician), no longer needed
- Services added: Travel clinic (12 years), ear wax, private services
- Dispensing model: independent (Safey's is 45+ year established pharmacy, Asif runs it day-to-day)

---

## The Before State
Safey's was on ProScript before Titan. At 15,000 items/month in a small corridor-sized pharmacy, it was "non stop, baskets piling up." Asif would rush between the consultation room and the dispensary: "I'd do a travel job in here, I'd run to the back in between, start checking, bagging, getting them on the shelf, and then running back for my next appointment — back and forth constantly." Mistakes happened. He was rushing checks, missing things. The counting bench was piled so high with baskets "I couldn't see across the room." Patients with high expectations in a competitive area would leave if orders weren't done next day.

## The Tipping Point
Asif first saw Titan at the Pharmacy Show a couple of years before buying it, but was locked into a ProScript contract. He knew immediately: "The moment I saw it, I thought, this is it. This is exactly what I need." When the contract expired, he also did a full pharmacy refit simultaneously — designing the dispensary layout specifically around Titan's workflow.

## What Changed
Titan eliminated the basket pile-up entirely. Barcode scanning removed the fear of staff errors: "I literally have no fears as to what my staff are doing, because I know that everything is being scanned." New staff pick it up within a week — "they've taken it like a duck to water." Asif no longer goes into the dispensary except for control drug checks or "a coffee every now and then." Titan AI (Titan X at the time, new version in beta) checks 30–40% of prescriptions; Asif expects the new version to reach 80–90%. Batch flow with single patient mode allows ~30–50 patients to be dispensed in one picking run without basket space constraints.

## The After State
Safey's grew from ~8,000 to 14,500–15,000 items. Asif can now spend proper time with patients during travel consultations — his Google reviews reflect the improvement in patient care. He has referred multiple pharmacy owner friends to Titan. Staff errors and "I haven't received this" patient calls are now definitively resolved with Titan's audit trail: batch number, expiry, dispenser name, time — "every single time they've called back and said, I found it." The pharmacy did a full refit to optimise the dispensary layout for Titan's workflow at the same time as switching.

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Asif is excited about the new Titan AI version reaching 80–90% clinical check coverage. He wants to lean into private services and continue growing. He references the pharmacy show moment as a pivotal point of clarity about where pharmacy was heading and why Titan was the answer.
