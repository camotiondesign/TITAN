## The Before State
Andy is rolling out Titan to his third pharmacy site, having already established it at two others. The previous PMR is not named. The onboarding and prep process shows Andy is systematic and experienced — the real "before" story is the contrast with what he observed at other pharmacies that weren't on Titan during difficult periods.

## The Tipping Point
Seeing what happened at the first two established Titan sites during the Easter period — three bank holidays, ~50 staff hours lost per week across the group, and a 10,000-item pharmacy that "didn't hit rope" and ran completely smoothly. The staff themselves attributed this to Titan.

## What Changed
The introduction of Titan batch flow specifically was cited as making a "huge difference" — being able to clear 1–2 hundred items with one staff member in the morning using a couple of trips around the dispensary. Andy now runs Titan training at a 70–95% knowledge transfer model, using onboarding videos first, then a trainer visit for the advanced layer.

## The After State
Andy's first two established Titan sites now run smoothly even through major disruption. His team is rolling out site three with confidence. He describes learning Titan basics as "like learning to use a Sainsbury's self checkout — really intuitive." His prep-first philosophy means trainer days deliver maximum value.

## Best Direct Quotes
- "Learning to use Titan at a basic level is like learning to use a Sainsbury's self checkout — it's really intuitive."
- "We had the Easter period where we've probably lost about 50 hours of staff per week, and we had three bank holidays in quick succession, and we're doing a 10,000 item pharmacy, and we didn't hit rope, and it literally went as smooth as it has ever been. And we put that — and the staff put that — down to Titan."
- "The introduction of batch bow has made a huge difference. Just being able to get all the 123-item scripts and get them done with just a couple of trips around the dispensary."
- "What you're really looking for from your trainer is to take people from knowing Titan 70% to taking them to 95%. That's where the trainer comes in."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Andy is systematically building out a multi-site operation on Titan, aiming to get all his pharmacies on the platform. He is structured, methodical, and focused on prep-driven implementation to get maximum value from training investment.
