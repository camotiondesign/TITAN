## The Before State
Anoop is a pharmacist and owner of a growing group. Before choosing Titan, they evaluated multiple PMR systems. The key frustration was that older systems kept the pharmacist locked into the dispensing process, making it impossible to scale services as pharmacy moved towards Pharmacy First and private offerings.

## The Tipping Point
Choosing Titan was a deliberate strategic decision to free pharmacist time ahead of pharmacy services expansion. The interview was captured on implementation day of their 8th Titan pharmacy.

## What Changed
Titan allows the workflow to be shared across the team, freeing pharmacist time. Anoop (a pharmacist themselves) notes that as a result, less dispensing work is coming their way and they can do more services.

## The After State
Anoop has 8 pharmacies on Titan and plans 3 more, aiming for Titan "all the way through." Services capacity has increased and pharmacist time has been freed up. No specific metrics given.

## Best Direct Quotes
- "We were looking at even other PMR systems — it was allowing us to see the benefit in the sense of like, allowed to follow the processes with the team working together. It allows us to free up pharmacist time."
- "As a pharmacist myself, you'd ever seen less work coming towards my way. So I'm able to do more services again, so it's working out pretty well."
- "We've got three more to go, and then hopefully we're Titan all the way through."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Complete Titan rollout across all 11+ pharmacies in the group. Use freed pharmacist time to grow NHS and private services as pharmacy evolves.
