## The Before State
Malia locums at other pharmacies and contrasts their paper-based systems with Titan. At paper-based pharmacies, every patient gets a 15–20 minute waiting time because you can't locate prescriptions instantly. At Drayton Prime before she was comfortable with Titan, she was staying late to get work done. She describes the bench filling up every hour with baskets she had to personally sign off — "it was boring and there was always that fear of like, have I checked that properly?"

## The Tipping Point
"I think it comes with experience. So the more experience you have on the system, the more relaxed you feel. And you don't have to have eyes towards the back of your head when you're using this system — whereas with ProScript you did."

## What Changed
Titan AI handles ~70% of clinical checking at Drayton Prime, meaning Malia can leave the dispensary for extended periods without the workload backing up. She can log in from home to check the inbox and delegate urgent items remotely. The audit trail means she knows exactly what happened, who did it, and when — without needing to ask staff. Batch flow processes 30 care homes at a time rather than one by one. The whole pharmacy workflow is "triple times faster" in her estimation.

## The After State
Malia finishes at 4:30 and goes home at 4:30 — "I wouldn't even stay for a minute extra." NMS went from near-zero while on ProScript to up to 300+ per month on Titan. Ear microsuction is now a regular service (30–45 minutes per patient), which wouldn't have been possible before. Titan AI does ~70% of clinical checks. Malia describes the pharmacy as "a very fun, vibrant place" that's "always looking for improving the services for not just the patient, but also staff members."

## Best Direct Quotes
- "I have locumed in other pharmacies and they were all paper based — if a patient came in, you would always give them waiting time of 15–20 minutes. Here you can locate the prescription straight away, and you can get it dispensed, checked and handed out within five minutes."
- "With proscript, I'd have my bench fill up probably every hour and I'd be signing up my bench right the whole day, just checking — which was boring, and there would always be that fear of like, have I checked that properly? Do I need to recheck it?"
- "I don't have to have eyes towards the back of your head when you're using this system, whereas with ProScript you did."
- "I finish at 4:30, I'd go home at 4:30. I wouldn't even stay for a minute extra."
- "Titan AI is doing about 70% of the clinical checking."
- "We barely did any NMS while we were on ProScript. But now with Titan, we can do up to 300 if not more than that, per month."
- "Titan has freed up a lot of space in terms of my workload on a daily basis, so I'm not glued to the screen all the time."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Malia sees Titan as enabling a pharmacy model where the system runs smoothly in the background regardless of her direct involvement. She can even work remotely from home when needed. The pharmacy's identity as a "fun, vibrant place" that's patient-focused and staff-focused is central to how she describes it.
