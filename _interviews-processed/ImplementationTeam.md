## Kieran (Easons Pharmacy, Tamworth) — Key Signals

### Quick Stats
- Items per month (before): not stated
- Services added: NMS 30 → 180/month
- Hours saved: working late 2–3 nights/week → home every night; working from home "several days a week"

### The Before State
Kieran was "working two or three nights a week late, stressed, burnt out" before Titan. Had services running but couldn't take on more capacity. Described himself as not having the capacity.

### The After State
NMS grew from 30 to 180/month. All other services (NHS and private) increased similarly. Staff run the dispensary — "they shout at me if I go in." Patients get text messages as soon as prescriptions are on the shelf. Works from home several days a week via remote access.

### Best Quotes (Kieran)
- "Before I was using Titan, I was working two or three nights a week late. I was stressed, burnt out. I was doing a decent number of services, but I couldn't take any more on. I just didn't have the capacity to do it."
- "Now I get home every night, on time. I even work from home several days a week if I like."
- "Our services are increased. Our NMS have gone from 30 to 180 a month, and all our other services, private and NHS, have all done likewise."
- "My staff run my dispensary. I don't go in it anymore. They shout at me if I go in."
- "If you're on the fence, go see a pharmacy. Check it out, see how it works for real. And then get ready to hand your notice in at your current PMR."

## Mustafa (Silverwood Pharmacy) — Key Signals

### The Before State
Mustafa was "very skeptical of Titan for years." Got a demo a couple of years earlier and felt "the software wasn't quite ready." Came back years later for the second branch and found it "leaps and bounds" from where it was.

### The After State
Persuaded by peer recommendations — other friends using Titan successfully. Core insight: "Titan has allowed us to do things we could never have done with the previous system. The whole process is a better process of doing prescriptions than what we used to have." Reduces dispensing errors to "virtually zero."

### Best Quotes (Mustafa)
- "For years since Titan was launched, I've been very skeptical of Titan... now it's come leaps and bounds from what I knew it to be in the beginning."
- "This system reduces down dispensing errors to virtually zero. And that is one of the most important things that any pharmacy would appreciate."
- "What Titan has allowed us to do, we could never have done it with the previous system."
- "You take on feedback from the users so that the programmers can then update the coding — no other PMR system does that."

## Nathan (Regional Manager, 22 Branches) — Key Signals

### Best Quotes (Nathan)
