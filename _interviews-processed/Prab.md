## The Before State
Prab bought Priory Pharmacy in 2021 — it had been under the same owner for ~40 years and was doing only 1,000 items per month, ticking along at retirement pace. Before Titan (at a previous pharmacy), workflow meant Mr. Jones would come in, a prescription would be on the system, then the pharmacist would check the paper file box, not find it there, check the dispensing bench, check the Owings — guesswork at every stage. ProScript was "archaic workflow" that holds pharmacy back.

## The Tipping Point
Prab was Titan customer number 2, having had the vision explained to him by Tariq and Wahid in Bristol before Titan launched. "I really thought, actually, this is going to be a real game changer and something I really want to do part of." He deliberately included the removal of the existing PMR in the purchase contract when buying Priory: "I made sure it was in my sales contract to say I will not be taking on this PMR. Literally took out the old ProScript PC and we dumped that around the back and Titan went in straight away."

## What Changed
Prab grew Priory from 1,000 to 16,000 items per month — a 16x increase — and credits Titan's ecosystem as the enabler. Batch flow (up to 5 simultaneous stations, up to 30 patient spaces each = 150 patients simultaneously) is the core capacity engine. Titan AI keeps prescriptions flowing without waiting for clinical checks. The entire dispensary layout was designed by Tariq specifically to maximise Titan workflow. Repeat flow transformed dosset patient management. Now installing a Meditech robot as the next step — chosen specifically because Titan's single basket mode integration makes robot + Titan smoother than robot + any other PMR.

## The After State
Priory is doing ~16,000 items/month. Prab can spend an hour or more with a visitor or patient without being interrupted. He estimates he'd need "3 or 4 extra staff members" without Titan to run the same volume. The pharmacy has solar panels + Tesla Powerwall (off-grid in summer), is 97–98% paperless, and won "Sustainable Pharmacy of the Year" in 2023. Prab is an independent prescriber building toward fully removing himself from the dispensing process once the robot is installed. Services include weight loss (taking off with GLP-1 boom), microsuction, travel, sexual health, and more.

## Best Direct Quotes
- "When I bought the pharmacy in 2021, it was only doing 1,000 items. So we've had really phenomenal growth in that time period, which is really thanks to Titan."
- "I often have people come visit the pharmacy and I like to show them how I work and to show them Titan, and they're just amazed how I can take an hour or an hour and a half out my day and have a conversation with them and not be disturbed."
- "If I was doing this volume with another PMR, I would probably need three or four members of staff at least."
- "One pharmacy once, when I was showing off Titan, said I was like 'the caveman seeing fire for the first time.' That really tickled me."
- "It's given me a bit more professional fulfillment, because I can focus more on services... but I think it gave me my work life balance back. Because if I was this busy and I had an old PMR, I'd probably still be checking baskets back in the pharmacy."
- "I made it really, really easy for the staff. I explain to them — whatever's on the left hand side, you need to get to the right hand side of the board. And in doing that, we've done the prescription as efficiently as possible."
- "I'm able to run my team leanly, but also in a safe manner, which I think is really crucial in this day and age."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Prab wants to fully detach from the dispensing process once the robot is installed — using his IP qualification to build a services-first business model. He plans to use Titanverse to grow private services consistently (weight loss, IP prescribing, travel). His goal: services revenue more steady month-to-month, not seasonal spikes.
