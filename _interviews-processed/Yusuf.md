## Quick Stats
- Items per month (before): variable across 11+ branches (all on ProScript)
- Items per month (after): ~170,000/month total across all branches
- Hours saved: pharmacist checking reduced from 100% → 20–30% in branches without ACT; services increased by 500% in some branches; 300% in those that had some services before
- Staff: ~150 total across all branches; directors are two brothers and a wife (all pharmacists); all branch staff known by name
- Services added: Pharmacy First, travel vaccinations, ear microsuction, weight management, contraception/hypertension case finding, NMS (centralized via Titanverse), private services growing; 500% increase in some branches
- Dispensing model: large independent group (BNP Healthcare); hub-and-spoke peer-to-peer being developed; directors and staff all pharmacists

## The Before State
All BNP stores ran on ProScript — "quick and efficient for labeling, very good in terms of accuracy, but at the end of it, I've got a mountain to check as a pharmacist." Without Titan, pharmacists were doing 100% of checking even in branches without ACTs, and services were "basically non-existent" in some branches due to time constraints. Yusuf attended the Pharmacy Show every year for 10 years and watched Titan develop, thinking "it's not quite ready" until 2023. The industry was "chasing items" — and BNP knew that model was changing.

## The Tipping Point
The 2023 Pharmacy Show. "At that time, Titan were the only ones who actually knew pharmacy were changing and sharing us stuff that we knew we needed to be a part of. We knew that Titan was going to be that platform." They put it in one smaller store to start, and "six months later we were adding on a branch a month."

## What Changed
Barcode scanning eliminated human error at the label-fixing stage — "one label at a time, one box at a time, you've completely eliminated the ability to make that error." Pharmacist checking dropped from 100% → 20–30% per branch. Services exploded: some branches 500% increase, others at least 300%. Staff feel empowered rather than dependent on pharmacist availability. Repeat flow automated the entire dosset patient prescription cycle — previously manual with diaries and drivers taking paper to surgeries. Titanverse enabled centralised NMS from head office (one pharmacist vs. needing extra pharmacists per site). AI on Titanverse creates consultation templates from scratch — "the AI is literally the greatest thing I've ever seen."

## The After State
BNP now does ~170,000 items/month. Pharmacists are planted in consultation rooms, not on the dispensing bench. Every branch has the audit trail: time, batch number, expiry, user login — "if there is ever a problem, it's all auditable down to the person." Services at 300–500% growth. Titanverse AI scribes consultations (voice transcription, auto-fills template, generates prescription straight to Titan board). Travel vaccination consultations are fully paperless and digital — from booking through to dispensing with no manual steps. Hub-and-spoke peer-to-peer is the next strategic layer being built. Staff have been retrained and upskilled — not cut.

## Best Direct Quotes
- "Imagine now that my pharmacist is able to basically sit in that room and have the time — and not be under the stress of knowing the workload is in the dispensary still for them to do. Our services have increased in some branches by 500%."
- "We haven't reduced our staffing levels. We're retraining our staff to be able to do different things — level-2 dispensers are able to do vaccinations — rather than looking to make cuts."
- "If all staff have to adhere to a strict process — user logins, smart card entry, barcode scanning of each individual item — the batch number, the expiry, it tells you who's done it. It's all auditable down to the person."
- "There's not one software provider that will do that in the pharmacy industry other than Titan — a WhatsApp group with the software developer, the head of sales, the owner, all listening to everyone's suggestions."
- "Within the next six months, using Titan, we're going to be looking at the hub-and-spoke peer-to-peer model. Now that we do about 170,000 items, that will be the next step of efficiency for us."
- "Five years ago, or even 10 years ago, the industry was chasing items. We're not chasing items anymore. We're about service."
- "Why haven't you already done it yet?" (What he'd say to a pharmacist not yet on Titan)
- **On Titanverse AI:** "The AI is literally the greatest thing I've ever seen. I couldn't believe — even in this day and age now with AI — how easy it is to be able to create templates for each and every service."
- "With Titanverse, I'm sitting facing the patient, looking at them, having a conversation with them. Because it's picking it all up, I don't need to sit there typing away afterwards."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Yusuf sees BNP as a "family-oriented independent pharmacy chain" where technology enables human-quality care at scale. The next strategic steps are hub-and-spoke peer-to-peer (170,000 items/month is the right scale for this), continued Titanverse adoption for AI-powered services, and potentially expanding the dispenser vaccination capability further. He describes Titanverse's AI as revolutionary for services — even as an experienced prescriber of 3 years, it surprised him with the level of clinical thoroughness it brings.
