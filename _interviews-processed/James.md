## The Before State
James primarily works the counter at Night Street Pharmacy. Before Titan (day one of implementation), finding prescriptions was a manual alphabetical search through filing boxes — time-consuming and unreliable for communicating prescription status to patients.

## The Tipping Point
Day one implementation — very short post-training testimonial. Not a structured before/after story.

## What Changed
The shelf location feature eliminated the manual search through filing boxes. Titan now tells James where the prescription is and whether it's been bagged/ready or still in process. The system also talks to James when he assigns deliveries — flagging delivery patients audibly. Batch flow gave dispensers a printed pick list in alphabetical order, dramatically speeding up picking. James can also order counter stock by scanning barcodes directly — "paperless" as he notes.

## The After State
James is positive on day one. "I'm liking it, yeah, definitely using it." No going back — "I don't have to take a pen and paper."

## Best Direct Quotes
- "Before you used to have alphabetical order, yeah, these ones. So you have to go through all the boxes to find the prescription. Time consuming."
- "You've got shelf location now, to make it easier to locate it — now I know whether it's been already bagged and ready to hand out, or it's still in the process."
- "It says delivery out loud, talks to you. Stuck in. Really easy."
- "I don't have to take a pen and paper. As Titan says, it's paperless."
