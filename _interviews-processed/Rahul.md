- Hours saved: pharmacist freed ~20 hours/week since implementing Titan in January 2025; "double the amount of appointments" now possible
- Staff: 2 pharmacists at flagship Southall; 1 pharmacist at Hillingdon; family-run for 40+ years
- Services added: Pharmacy First, earwax removal (microsuction), phlebotomy, travel vaccine clinics, weight loss clinics, private medical cannabis clinic; becoming a "community health hub"
- Dispensing model: independent, multi-site family business (40+ year established)

## The Before State
Puri Pharmacy ran on ProScript before Titan (implemented January 2025). Before Titan, Rahul and his team were "frantically having to check all day" — using paper, pens, diaries, scribbling things down to manage repeat prescriptions, dosset patients, and Owings. Services like Pharmacy First and private clinics existed before Titan but in limited form because pharmacists were "stuck to the bench." "Two years ago, we were frantically using paper diaries, post it notes, prescriptions everywhere." Proscript was described as an "archaic system."

## The Tipping Point
Rahul visited a pharmacy using Titan for a site visit and realised it was the future. "When someone said they could relieve all of this, I thought, you're pulling my leg. But then I went for a site visit, and I realised that the future is Titan. After one site visit, I picked up the phone and I ordered the system." Implemented January 2025.

## What Changed
Titan AI and repeat flow were described as "revolutionary." Pharmacists no longer need to be at the bench — Titan AI handles clinical checking, allowing Rahul to be in the consultation room doing services without being disturbed. Repeat flow eliminated the manual chasing of dosset patient prescriptions (previously: paper diary, monthly manual effort). Text notifications mean patients receive their bag confirmation within a few hours of the pharmacy receiving the prescription — without staff manual effort. Everything is visible on a shared screen — every team member knows the workflow status.

## The After State
Puri Pharmacy is now a "paper free pharmacy." Services have expanded significantly: Pharmacy First, earwax, phlebotomy, travel, weight loss, private cannabis clinic. "Since implementing Titan in January 2025, it's freed up at least 20 hours a week for a pharmacist." Services have "increased by double" in terms of appointments possible. The pharmacy is planning a full renovation at one branch because they can no longer accommodate the volume of service appointments Titan has made possible — they need 2–3 more consultation rooms. Both branches are now looking to expand and go deeper into digital/telehealth. Rahul wants to be the "Apple Store" of pharmacy — a holistic diagnostic health hub.

- "Titan has given me the freedom to lead from the front — both clinically, commercially and creatively."
- "If I had to summarise Prairie pharmacy in one sentence: a modern health hub that blends clinical care, digital innovation and deep community roots."

## Broader Vision / Ambition
Rahul wants to build a full holistic health ecosystem — combining Titan's dispensary management with telehealth, remote IP prescribing, digital tools (DemDX, AcuRX), and eventually expanding nationally through remote services. He is building the "Apple Store of pharmacy" — a community health hub model that Titan's freed capacity makes possible. Plans include 2–3 more consultation rooms, more pharmacists, and eventually a digital-first/bricks-and-mortar hybrid business.
