## Notes for Implementation

- **Hook Structure:** All hooks follow the 3-line pattern (statement, break, twist/reveal)
- **Tone:** Calm confidence, constructive ambition, grounded in UK pharmacy reality
- **No Hype:** Avoided "revolutionising," "game-changing," empty marketing phrases
- **Specific:** Concrete examples (paper forms, WhatsApp threads, NMS, IP clinics)
- **Truth:** Acknowledges the pressure, doesn't promise easy fixes
- **Partnership:** "The pharmacies that adapt early will define what it becomes"
