# Infographic Design Prompt: Titan PMR to Titanverse Pathway

## Campaign Context
- **Purpose:** Visualize the pathway/transition from Titan PMR (dispensing efficiency) to Titanverse (service delivery)
- **Brand:** TITANVERSE (with Titan PMR connection)
- **Target Audience:** Titan PMR customers, Pharmacy owners considering service expansion, Superintendents
- **Date:** 2025-12-10
- **Format:** Single image infographic (can be used for LinkedIn, blog, or carousel slide)

## Design Reference
- **Titan PMR Colors:** Gradient `#2DA7FF` (light blue) → `#2D40FF` (darker blue)
- **Titanverse Colors:** Gradient `#FF65DD` (pink) → `#B752FF` (purple)
- **Supporting Colors:** White, Black
- **Visual Style:** Reference `/design-reference/titan-pmr-visual-style.md` and `/design-reference/titanverse-visual-style.md`

## Concept 1: "The Pathway from Titan PMR to Titanverse"

### Visual Approach: Pathway/Flow Diagram

**Layout:**
- Horizontal or vertical pathway showing progression
- Left side: Titan PMR (blue gradient)
- Right side: Titanverse (purple gradient)
- Connecting pathway/flow in between

**Visual Elements:**

**Left Side - Titan PMR:**
- Icon/visual representing dispensing efficiency
- Key elements:
  - Prescription baskets/items
  - AI dispensing features
  - Batch flow/Kanban
  - Barcode safety
  - Mobile access
- Text: "Titan PMR" in blue gradient
- Supporting text: "Dispensing Efficiency" or "Freed from Dispensing"

**Pathway/Connection:**
- Flowing arrow or pathway connecting left to right
- Can be:
  - Curved arrow
  - Stepped pathway
  - Flowing line with transition elements
- Color: Gradient from blue to purple (showing transition)
- Optional: Small icons/elements along pathway showing:
  - "Capacity Created"
  - "Time Freed"
  - "Efficiency Gained"

**Right Side - Titanverse:**
- Icon/visual representing service delivery
- Key elements:
  - Consultation interface
  - Service icons (NMS, Travel Health, IP, Private)
  - AI features (templates, transcription, agents)
  - Calendar/bookings
- Text: "Titanverse" in purple gradient
- Supporting text: "Service Delivery" or "Services Platform"

**Overall Message:**
- Clear visual progression from one to the other
- Shows natural evolution/next step
- Not side-by-side comparison, but pathway/flow

**Typography:**
- Headline: "The Pathway from Titan PMR to Titanverse" (bold, large)
- Supporting text: Clear, readable
- Use brand colors for product names

**Background:**
- Clean white or light gradient
- Optional: Subtle pathway/flow elements in background

## Concept 2: "How Titan PMR Customers Transition"

### Visual Approach: Step-by-Step Transition Process

**Layout:**
- Vertical or horizontal step-by-step process
- Numbered or icon-based steps
- Clear progression from start to finish

**Visual Elements:**

**Step 1: "You're Already Using Titan PMR"**
- Icon: Titan PMR logo/interface
- Visual: Dispensing workflow
- Color: Blue gradient
- Text: "Dispensing efficiency achieved"

**Step 2: "Capacity Created"**
- Icon: Clock/time or capacity meter
- Visual: Time freed, efficiency gained
- Color: Transition (blue to purple gradient)
- Text: "Time and capacity for services"

**Step 3: "Add Titanverse"**
- Icon: Plus/connection symbol
- Visual: Titanverse logo/interface
- Color: Purple gradient
- Text: "Services platform added"

**Step 4: "Run Services"**
- Icon: Consultation/service icons
- Visual: Service delivery in action
- Color: Purple gradient
- Text: "Deliver clinical and private services"

**Step 5: "One Ecosystem"**
- Icon: Connected/ecosystem visual
- Visual: Both platforms working together
- Color: Combined blue and purple
- Text: "PMR + Services in one ecosystem"

**Connections:**
- Arrows or flow lines between steps
- Clear progression visual
- Can be circular (ecosystem) or linear (journey)

**Overall Message:**
- Natural progression/transition
- Easy, logical steps
- No disruption, just addition

**Typography:**
- Headline: "How Titan PMR Customers Transition" (bold, large)
- Step labels: Clear, numbered or icon-based
- Supporting text: Readable, concise

**Background:**
- Clean white or light gradient
- Optional: Subtle flow elements

## Design Principles

### Color Usage
- **Titan PMR:** Blue gradient (`#2DA7FF` → `#2D40FF`)
- **Titanverse:** Purple gradient (`#FF65DD` → `#B752FF`)
- **Transition/Connection:** Gradient from blue to purple
- **Background:** White or light gradient
- **Text:** Black or white (depending on background)

### Visual Style
- **Clean and Modern:** Professional, uncluttered
- **Clear Flow:** Easy to follow progression
- **Icon-Based:** Use clear, recognizable icons
- **Brand Consistent:** Maintain brand identity
- **Avoid:** Side-by-side before/after (use flow/pathway instead)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, large, readable
- **Body Text:** Clean sans-serif
- **Brand Names:** Use brand colors
- **Hierarchy:** Clear visual hierarchy
