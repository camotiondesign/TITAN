# Titanverse Visual Style Guide

## Overview

Titanverse uses a modern, clean illustration style with a pastel color palette. Visuals emphasize transformation, efficiency, and comprehensive service management.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Brand Gradient
- **Titanverse Gradient**: `#FF65DD` (bright pink/magenta) to `#B752FF` (purple)
- **Usage**: Primary brand identity, backgrounds, key visual elements, text highlights
- **Direction**: Can be used vertically, horizontally, or diagonally
- **Examples**: Logo text ("VERSE" in gradient), quote highlights, sparkle icons, star elements

### Supporting Colors
- **White**: Clean backgrounds, text, clothing elements, documents
- **Black**: Typography, text elements, strong contrast
- **Deep Purple/Black**: Dark backgrounds for cosmic/space theme

### Color Usage
- **Backgrounds**: 
  - Dark purple-to-black gradient (cosmic theme, most common)
  - White (for clean, professional look)
  - Purple gradient (for emphasis)
- **Text**: 
  - White (primary text on dark backgrounds)
  - Purple-to-pink gradient (for emphasis, highlights, brand elements)
  - Black (on light backgrounds)
- **Visual Elements**: 
  - Purple-to-pink gradient for icons, stars, sparkles
  - White for contrast and clarity
  - Black for strong emphasis

---

## Typography

### Headlines
- **Font**: Bold, sans-serif
- **Style**: Uppercase for emphasis (most common)
- **Size**: Large, prominent
- **Color**: 
  - White (on dark purple backgrounds - most common)
  - Pink-to-purple gradient (for emphasis, brand elements)
  - Black (on light backgrounds)
- **Examples**: "THANK YOU TO EVERYONE WHO JOINED THE FIRST WEBINAR!", "From noise", "To calm", "Dispensing-Led Pharmacy", "Service-Led Health Hub"

### Body Text
- **Font**: Clean sans-serif
- **Style**: Readable, professional
- **Color**: 
  - White (on dark backgrounds)
  - Grey/white (on light backgrounds)
  - Black (on white backgrounds)
- **Vertical Text**: Used on service bars (bottom to top reading)

### Text on Bars
- **Format**: Vertical text along bars
- **Color**: White text on colored bars
- **Style**: Bold, sans-serif

### Brand Text
- **"TITANVERSE"**: 
  - "TITAN" in white
  - "VERSE" in purple-to-pink gradient
  - Bold, uppercase sans-serif
- **Logo**: Large purple four-pointed star above brand name

## Layout Patterns

### Side-by-Side Comparison
- **Structure**: Vertical split (left/right panels)
- **Use Case**: Before/After, Old Model/New Model
- **Elements**: 
  - Title at top of each panel
  - Main graphic (bars, charts) in center
  - Supporting graphics/icons below
  - Figure/character positioned to side
- **Example**: "Before" (single bar) vs "Now" (multiple bars)

### Bar Chart Layout
- **Structure**: Multiple vertical bars of varying heights
- **Arrangement**: Left to right, increasing height
- **Colors**: Different color per bar/service
- **Text**: Vertical labels on each bar
- **Use Case**: Service diversification, revenue mix, growth visualization

### Central Figure Layout
- **Structure**: Central character/figure with surrounding icons
- **Elements**: 
  - Main figure in center
  - Icons/elements positioned around or held by figure
  - Clean background with subtle decorative elements
- **Use Case**: Efficiency, multitasking, comprehensive management

## Visual Elements

### Characters/Figures
- **Style**: Modern, friendly, professional
- **Diversity**: Represent different people (light skin, dark skin, various hair styles)
- **Clothing**: 
  - Professional pantsuits in purple (Titanverse) or teal-green (comparison visuals)
  - White t-shirts, white ID badges on lanyards
  - White sneakers with purple/teal accents
- **Pose**: 
  - Passive/neutral (old model - "Dispensing-Led Pharmacy")
  - Confident, professional (new model - hand on hip, slight smile - "Service-Led Health Hub")
- **Expression**: Neutral to friendly smile
- **Real Photos**: For testimonials - professional headshots on dark purple backgrounds

### Icons
- **Style**: Circular and rectangular icons with colored borders
- **Colors**: Purple-to-pink gradient borders, purple/grey internal elements
- **Types**:
  - Pill bottles with Rx symbol (white on purple background)
  - Dollar signs ($) and checkmarks (âœ“) on purple money bag
  - Calendars with grids (purple)
  - Gears and circuit boards (AI insights)
  - Brain icons (purple, representing intelligence/AI)
  - Prescription pads
  - Four-pointed stars/sparkles (purple-to-pink gradient)
- **Layout**: Held by figures or positioned around them
- **Background Icons**: System logos (Stripe, Pabau, Heidi, Tandem, Semble, Cliniko, Worldpay) for "noise" visuals

### Decorative Elements
- **Stars/Sparkles**: Four-pointed stars in purple-to-pink gradient
- **Background Stars**: Small white/purple specks on dark backgrounds (cosmic theme)
- **Overlapping Circles**: Translucent, overlapping circles in light purple, light blue (on light backgrounds)
- **Purpose**: Add depth, cosmic/space theme, visual interest
- **Style**: Modern, futuristic, subtle

### Service Bars
- **Shape**: Vertical rectangular bars
- **Colors**: Vary by service (purple gradient, teal-green, pink, blue)
- **Text**: Vertical labels (bottom to top)
- **Height**: Varies to show importance/growth
- **Use Case**: Represent different services, revenue streams, capabilities
- **Example**: "Dispensing-Led" (single blue bar) vs "Service-Led Health Hub" (multiple bars: NHS Clinical Services in teal-green, Private Services in purple gradient, Dispensing in blue)

## Visual Style Characteristics

### Illustration Style
- **Type**: Flat, modern illustration
- **Detail Level**: Simplified, clean
- **Shading**: Minimal, flat colors
- **Lines**: Clean, defined edges

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Mood**: Futuristic, cosmic, innovative, modern
- **Tone**: Transformation, comprehensive solutions, "everything app"
- **Complexity**: Clean and uncluttered, but with cosmic/space elements
- **Focus**: Clear visual hierarchy, strong contrast (dark backgrounds, bright text)

## Top Performer Visual Patterns

### Comparison Visuals (High Performance)
- **Pattern**: Side-by-side Before/After
- **Performance**: Proven in blog image (1,539 impressions, 5.5% engagement)
- **Elements**: 
  - Single tall bar (old) vs multiple balanced bars (new)
  - Passive figure (old) vs confident professional (new)
  - Color coding (red = problem, green = solution)

### Multi-Service Visualization
- **Pattern**: Multiple bars representing different services
- **Use Case**: Service diversification, revenue mix
- **Visual**: Bars of varying heights, different colors per service

### Efficiency/Comprehensive Management
- **Pattern**: Central figure with multiple elements/icons
- **Theme**: Multitasking, comprehensive oversight
- **Visual**: Multi-armed figure or figure surrounded by service icons

## Usage Guidelines

### When to Use This Style

**Titanverse Brand:**
- Service-focused content
- Transformation narratives
- Efficiency and comprehensive management messaging
- Multi-service visualization
- Before/After comparisons

**Visual Applications:**
- Carousel slides showing service evolution
- Single images showing service diversification
- Infographics comparing models
- Service capability showcases

### What Works

- **Purple-to-Pink Gradient**: Use for brand identity, highlights, key elements
- **Dark Backgrounds**: Deep purple-to-black gradients for cosmic theme
- **White Text**: Strong contrast on dark backgrounds
- **Bar Charts**: Multiple bars show service diversification effectively
- **Character Diversity**: Represent different people authentically
- **Cosmic Elements**: Stars, sparkles, space theme reinforce "cosmic console" positioning
- **Professional Figures**: Confident, modern, approachable (purple suits, ID badges)

### What to Avoid

- **Cluttered Layouts**: Keep it clean and focused
- **Too Many Colors**: Stick to purple-to-pink gradient, white, black
- **Complex Illustrations**: Keep figures and icons simple
- **Light Backgrounds for Hero Content**: Prefer dark purple gradients for main content (cosmic theme)
- **Overly Stylized**: Maintain professional, approachable aesthetic
- **Mixing Brand Colors**: Don't use Titan blue gradient in Titanverse content

## Technical Specifications

### File Formats
- **Illustrations**: Vector (SVG, AI) preferred, high-res PNG for raster
- **Icons**: Vector format for scalability
- **Final Export**: PNG for social media, maintain source files

### Dimensions
- **Carousel Slides**: 1080x1080px (LinkedIn)
- **Single Images**: Platform-specific (LinkedIn: 1200x627px, etc.)
- **Icons**: Scalable vector format

### Accessibility
- **Contrast**: Ensure text meets WCAG AA standards
- **Alt Text**: Always provide detailed alt text descriptions
- **Color Independence**: Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning
