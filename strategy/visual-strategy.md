# Visual Strategy

## Principle
Distinctive visual identity builds authority.

Generic healthcare imagery gets ignored.

Every campaign should have a recognisable visual world.

---

## Design Rules

Bold typography.

Clear visual hierarchy.

One idea per slide.

Minimal text.

Readable on mobile.

---

## Campaign Visual Worlds

Automation campaigns:
Clean dispensaries and operational flow visuals.

AI campaigns:
Abstract network or intelligence visuals.

Consultation campaigns:
Human clinical environments.

Governance campaigns:
Process diagrams and frameworks.

---

## Carousel Structure

Slide 1:
The problem.

Slide 2:
Why the problem matters.

Slides 3‑5:
System explanation.

Slide 6:
Proof.

Slide 7:
Takeaway.
