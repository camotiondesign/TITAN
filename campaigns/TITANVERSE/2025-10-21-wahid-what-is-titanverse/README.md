# Wahid What is Titanverse

Product education video featuring Wahid introducing Titanverse platform through talking head + animated explainer format. Explains how Titanverse solves fragmentation in pharmacy services and showcases key features.

## Primary Asset

- Short clip (talking head + animated explainer)

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post (boosted)

## Strategic Note

Product education video effectively introduced Titanverse platform. Strong organic performance (11.7% engagement rate) indicates high interest. Comments from Gareth Hughes, Michael Holden, and Qamar Hussain validate positioning and generate social proof. Boosted post significantly increased reach (9,831 sponsored impressions).

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** October 21, 2025  
**Product:** Titanverse  
**Type:** Product Education  
**Purpose:** Introduce Titanverse platform through Wahid's explainer video, educating pharmacy professionals on Titanverse capabilities and positioning it as the unified platform for pharmacy services.

## Core Message

Titanverse is the new platform we built specifically for pharmacy services. It brings everything together and uses AI to take the pressure off.
