## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** Wahid What is Titanverse  
**Date Range:** 2025-10-21 to 2025-11-03  
**Product:** TITANVERSE  
**Campaign Type:** Product Education

**Objective:**
Introduce Titanverse platform through Wahid's explainer video, educating pharmacy professionals on Titanverse capabilities and positioning it as the unified platform for pharmacy services.

- Short clip (talking head + animated explainer)

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Product education format:** Talking head + animated explainer effectively introduced Titanverse platform
- **Strong organic performance:** 11.7% organic engagement rate indicates high interest in product education content
- **Boosted post effective:** 9,831 sponsored impressions significantly expanded reach
- **Good video performance:** 512 views with 16-second average watch time demonstrates engaging explainer format
- **Strong comments:** Comments from Gareth Hughes ("Such an exciting development"), Michael Holden ("Every pharmacy should have a clinical management system"), and Qamar Hussain ("This is what you call 'next level'") validate Titanverse positioning
- **Feature showcase:** Video effectively showcased key features (AI templates, AI scribe, digital prescribing, clinical pathways, bookings, payments, etc.)

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited sponsored engagement:** 17 sponsored engagements from 9,831 impressions (0.17% engagement rate) suggests sponsored audience was less engaged
- **Low sponsored CTR:** 9 clicks from 9,831 sponsored impressions (0.09% CTR) indicates sponsored content didn't drive clicks effectively
- **Limited comments:** Only 3 comments despite 136 total engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion

### Audience Insights

- **Product education resonates:** Strong organic performance (11.7% engagement rate) indicates high interest in product education content
- **Boosted reach effective:** 9,831 sponsored impressions significantly expanded audience
- **Sponsored audience less engaged:** Low sponsored engagement rate (0.17%) suggests sponsored audience was less interested than organic audience
- **Video format effective:** 16-second average watch time demonstrates engaging explainer format
- **Strong validation:** Comments from pharmacy professionals validate Titanverse positioning

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue product education format:** Talking head + animated explainer effectively introduces platform
- **Optimize sponsored targeting:** Low sponsored engagement suggests need for better audience targeting
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose explainer into carousel with feature breakdown
- **Create explainer series:** Build series of explainer videos for different features

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Product education format, talking head + animated explainer, feature showcase, clear messaging
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, feature breakdown, explainer series, stronger CTA
- **Test:** Different explainer angles, multiple feature focuses, carousel format

### Process Improvements

- **Product education workflow:** Develop process for creating product education content
- **Explainer content library:** Build library of explainer content for future use
- **Sponsored targeting strategy:** Develop strategy for optimizing sponsored audience targeting
- **Product education template:** Create template for product education content with proven structure
