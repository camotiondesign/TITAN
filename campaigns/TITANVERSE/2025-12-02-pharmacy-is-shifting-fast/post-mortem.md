# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** Pharmacy is Shifting Fast  
**Date Range:** 2025-12-02  
**Product:** TITANVERSE  
**Campaign Type:** Product Positioning

**Objective:**
Position Titanverse as the unified console solution for pharmacy services shift, emphasizing revenue opportunity in services and demonstrating how Titanverse consolidates entire workflow into one place.

**Key Assets:**
- Carousel (2 slides)
- LinkedIn post

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Revenue opportunity messaging:** "The money is in services" effectively communicated business value
- **Comprehensive platform showcase:** Listing all capabilities (prescribing, booking, AI templates, CRM, SOAP notes, referrals, payments, ambient transcription) demonstrated platform comprehensiveness
- **EXCELLENT engagement:** 22.4% engagement rate and 18.9% CTR indicate extremely strong interest in unified console solution
- **Carousel format:** 2-slide carousel successfully communicated value proposition
- **High click volume:** 130 clicks from 687 impressions demonstrates exceptional engagement quality
- **Valuable comments:** Comments from Manuel Juban Jr. ("game changer") and Abhyuday Daharwal ("innovative solutions") validate positioning
- **Follower growth:** 4 followers gained indicates content successfully attracted new audience

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited reposts:** Only 4 reposts despite excellent engagement suggests content was valuable but not highly shareable
- **Limited page views:** Only 5 page views suggests content didn't drive significant website traffic despite high CTR
- **Moderate reach:** 687 impressions is moderate compared to other campaigns

### Audience Insights

- **Services shift resonates:** EXCELLENT engagement (22.4% engagement rate) indicates audience highly values services shift messaging
- **Unified console extremely valuable:** 18.9% CTR demonstrates extremely high intent to learn more about unified console solution
- **Revenue opportunity important:** "The money is in services" messaging resonated strongly with audience
- **Comprehensive platform valuable:** Listing all capabilities demonstrated platform comprehensiveness
- **Comments validate positioning:** Manuel Juban Jr. and Abhyuday Daharwal comments validate unified console positioning
