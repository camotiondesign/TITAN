# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** One App to Rule Them All Meme  
**Date Range:** 2025-11-18  
**Product:** TITANVERSE  
**Campaign Type:** Brand Momentum

**Objective:**
Position Titanverse as the unified platform solution through humorous LOTR-themed meme, engaging audience with pop culture reference while communicating "one app to rule them all" value proposition.

**Key Assets:**
- Single image (meme)
- LinkedIn post

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Meme format:** Humorous LOTR-themed meme successfully engaged audience with pop culture reference
- **Pop culture reference:** Lord of the Rings theme resonated with pharmacy audience
- **Good engagement:** 5.6% engagement rate and 4.0% CTR indicate meme format worked well
- **Strong follower growth:** 6 followers gained indicates content attracted new audience
- **Page views:** 13 page viewers suggest content drove website interest
- **Value proposition clear:** "One app to rule them all" effectively communicated unified platform positioning
- **Valuable comment:** Michael Holden FRPharmS FRSPH's comment reinforces clinical management system importance and validates unified platform positioning

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 80 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Limited reposts:** Only 3 reposts suggests content was valuable but not highly shareable
- **Moderate CTR:** 4.0% CTR is good but could be improved with stronger CTA

### Audience Insights

- **Meme format resonates:** Good engagement (5.6% engagement rate) indicates pharmacy audience appreciates humor
- **Pop culture works:** LOTR theme successfully engaged audience while communicating value proposition
- **Unified platform valuable:** 4.0% CTR suggests strong interest in unified platform solution
- **Follower growth:** 6 followers gained indicates content successfully attracted new audience
- **Website interest:** 13 page viewers suggest content drove website traffic

## Recommendations

- **Continue meme format:** Meme format successfully engaged audience with humor while communicating value proposition
