# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** AI in Pharmacy Interview  
**Date Range:** 2025-12-03  
**Product:** TITANVERSE  
**Campaign Type:** Thought Leadership

**Objective:**
Address AI fear in pharmacy sector through customer interview, positioning AI as tool that unlocks pharmacy potential rather than threat, demonstrating real-world benefits through user testimony.

**Key Assets:**
- Short video (customer interview)
- LinkedIn post

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Thought leadership approach:** Addressing AI fear directly through customer testimony effectively positioned AI as tool rather than threat
- **Customer testimony:** Real user testimony about AI benefits (efficiency, accuracy, service improvement) provided credibility
- **Good engagement:** 9.1% engagement rate and 7.1% CTR indicate strong interest in AI positioning content
- **Video performance:** 397 views with 17-second average watch time demonstrates engaging interview format
- **Reposts indicate interest:** 5 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Follower growth:** 2 followers gained indicates content attracted new audience
- **Valuable comment:** Abhyuday Daharwal's comment validates AI potential messaging

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 2 comments despite 104 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Moderate reach:** 1,147 impressions is moderate compared to other campaigns
- **Limited page views:** Only 6 page views suggests content didn't drive significant website traffic

### Audience Insights

- **AI positioning resonates:** Good engagement (9.1% engagement rate) indicates audience values AI positioning content
- **Customer testimony valuable:** Real user testimony provided credibility and addressed fear
- **Fear addressing important:** Directly addressing AI fear resonated with audience
- **Video format effective:** 17-second average watch time demonstrates engaging interview format
- **Reposts indicate shareability:** 5 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with colleagues

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue thought leadership:** Addressing AI fear directly through customer testimony effectively positioned AI
- **Emphasize customer testimony:** Continue using real user testimony to address concerns and demonstrate value
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Create interview series:** Build series of customer interviews addressing different AI concerns
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose interview into carousel with key quotes and benefits

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Thought leadership approach, customer testimony, fear addressing, AI positioning messaging
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, use case examples, stronger CTA, carousel format
- **Test:** Different interview angles, multiple customer perspectives, carousel format

### Process Improvements

- **Thought leadership workflow:** Develop process for creating thought leadership content
- **Interview library:** Build library of customer interviews for future use
- **Fear addressing template:** Create template for addressing sector concerns with proven structure
- **Interview series planning:** Develop strategy for customer interview content series
