## Carousel Structure

**Narrative Flow:**
1. **Hook (POV Start):** You start a consultation
2. **Template Selection:** Choose template or create with AI
3. **Voice & Auto-fill:** Titanverse listens and auto-fills
4. **AI Chat:** Chat with AI for guidance anytime
5. **Automated Actions:** Hand off busy work to AI
6. **Summary:** Clean structured summary at the end

**Visual Style:**
- POV (first-person) perspective throughout
- Step-by-step consultation workflow
- Clear progression from start to finish
- AI features highlighted at each step
- Modern, clean design aesthetic

**Key Messages:**
- Less admin. More clarity. More care.
- Complete consultation workflow from start to finish
- AI support at every step
- Structured, organized output
