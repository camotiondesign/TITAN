## Learnings

### What Worked

- **POV format:** POV carousel format effectively communicated step-by-step consultation process from user perspective
- **Step-by-step narrative:** Clear progression from starting consultation to structured summary resonated with audience
- **EXCEPTIONAL engagement:** 76.8% engagement rate and 73.8% CTR indicate extremely strong interest in consultation workflow
- **Visual storytelling:** Carousel slides effectively demonstrated each step of consultation process
- **AI feature showcase:** Highlighted AI-powered features (voice transcription, auto-fill, AI chat, automated follow-ups) throughout flow
- **Valuable comment:** Sukhy Uppal's comment expressing excitement after seeing at Pharmacy Show validates product interest and generates social proof
- **Follower growth:** 4 followers gained indicates content successfully attracted new audience

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 697 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Limited reposts:** Only 4 reposts despite exceptional engagement suggests content was valuable but not highly shareable
- **Limited page views:** Only 1 page viewer suggests content didn't drive significant website traffic despite high CTR

### Audience Insights

- **Consultation workflow extremely valuable:** EXCEPTIONAL engagement (76.8% engagement rate) indicates audience highly values consultation workflow demonstration
