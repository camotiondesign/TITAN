# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** Titanverse Early Adopters: Yusuf  
**Date Range:** 2025-11-20 to 2025-11-25  
**Product:** TITANVERSE  
**Campaign Type:** Case Study

**Objective:**
Showcase Yusuf from BMP Healthcare as one of the earliest Titanverse adopters, demonstrating real clinical outcomes through AI-powered consultations, templates, transcription, and centralized servicing.

**Key Assets:**
- Single image testimonial (Nov 20)
- Short product review video (Nov 25)
- LinkedIn posts (2)

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Early adopter positioning:** Showcasing Yusuf as early adopter effectively demonstrated real-world outcomes
- **Multi-asset approach:** Testimonial + video combination provided comprehensive story
- **Good engagement:** 7.6% and 9.62% engagement rates indicate strong interest in early adopter stories
- **Strong CTR:** 4.8% and 6.53% click-through rates suggest case study link drove significant interest
- **Video performance:** 581 views demonstrates engaging testimonial format
- **Valuable comments:** Nas Khaliq's comment validates journey and provides strong social proof
- **High reposts:** 8 reposts on video indicate content was valuable for sharing

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments on testimonial:** Zero comments despite 91 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **Moderate reposts on testimonial:** Only 3 reposts suggests testimonial was less shareable than video
- **Limited follower growth:** Only 2 followers gained suggests content didn't attract significant new audience

### Audience Insights

- **Early adopter stories resonate:** Good engagement (7.6% and 9.62% engagement rates) indicates audience values early adopter validation
- **Video format more engaging:** Higher engagement rate (9.62% vs 7.6%) and more reposts (8 vs 3) suggest video format more effective
- **Case study links drive clicks:** Strong CTR (4.8% and 6.53%) suggests case study links drove significant interest
- **Journey validation important:** Nas Khaliq's comment highlighting 3-year journey validates long-term customer relationship
- **Social proof valuable:** "If you want change, this is how you do it" comment provides strong social proof

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue early adopter focus:** Early adopter stories effectively demonstrate real-world outcomes
- **Prioritize video format:** Video format generated higher engagement and more reposts
- **Emphasize journey:** Continue highlighting customer journey from Titan PMR to Titanverse
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Create adopter series:** Build series of early adopter case studies

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Early adopter positioning, testimonial format, video format, case study links, journey emphasis
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, use case examples, stronger CTA, carousel format
- **Test:** Different testimonial angles, multiple adopter stories, carousel format

### Process Improvements

- **Early adopter workflow:** Develop process for creating early adopter case study content
- **Testimonial library:** Build library of early adopter testimonials for future use
- **Case study template:** Create template for early adopter case studies with proven structure
- **Video series planning:** Develop strategy for early adopter video series
