# Titanverse Early Adopters: Yusuf

Case study campaign showcasing Yusuf from BMP Healthcare as one of the earliest Titanverse adopters. Multi-asset campaign demonstrating real clinical outcomes through AI-powered consultations, templates, transcription, and centralized servicing.

## Primary Assets

- Single image testimonial (Nov 20, 2025)
- Short product review video (Nov 25, 2025)

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn posts (2)

## Strategic Note

Early adopter case study effectively demonstrated real-world Titanverse outcomes. Good engagement on both assets (7.6% and 9.62% engagement rates) indicates strong interest in early adopter stories. Comment from Nas Khaliq validates journey from Titan PMR to Titanverse. Case study link drove significant clicks (57 and 93 clicks respectively). Video performance (581 views) demonstrates engaging testimonial format.
