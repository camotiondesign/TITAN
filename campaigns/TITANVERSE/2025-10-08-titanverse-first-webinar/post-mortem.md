## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Post-webinar thank you format:** Thank you post effectively continued event promotion momentum
- **Strong follower growth:** 11 followers gained indicates content attracted new audience
- **High reposts:** 7 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing and event awareness
- **Strong comments:** Comments from Hooman Ghalamkari, Phil Beard, and Sajid Ramzan validate Titanverse positioning
- **Social proof:** Comments generate social proof and validate "game changer" positioning
- **Continued countdown:** "4 days left" continued countdown momentum from previous post

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 3 comments despite 89 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Could add webinar highlights:** Missing specific webinar takeaways or highlights

### Audience Insights

- **Post-webinar content resonates:** Good engagement (8.3%) indicates strong interest in post-webinar content
- **Follower growth indicates interest:** 11 followers gained suggests content attracted new audience interested in Titanverse
- **Reposts build awareness:** 7 reposts indicate content was valuable for sharing and event awareness
- **Strong validation:** Comments from Hooman Ghalamkari, Phil Beard, and Sajid Ramzan validate Titanverse positioning

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue post-webinar format:** Post-webinar thank you posts effectively continue event promotion momentum
- **Add webinar highlights:** Include specific webinar takeaways or highlights to add value
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose thank you into carousel with webinar highlights
- **Create webinar series:** Build series of post-webinar content with highlights and takeaways

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Post-webinar thank you format, continued countdown, clear event details, brand messaging
- **Add:** Webinar highlights, discussion prompts, webinar takeaways, stronger CTA
- **Test:** Carousel format, multiple thank you angles, webinar preview content

### Process Improvements

- **Post-webinar workflow:** Develop process for creating post-webinar content
- **Webinar content library:** Build library of webinar highlights and takeaways
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Post-webinar template:** Create template for post-webinar content with proven structure

## Next Steps
