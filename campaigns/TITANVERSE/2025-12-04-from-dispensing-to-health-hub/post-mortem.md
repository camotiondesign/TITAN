# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** From Dispensing to Health Hub  
**Date Range:** 2025-12-04 to 2025-12-05  
**Product:** TITANVERSE / TITAN PMR  
**Campaign Type:** Positioning / Thought Leadership

**Objective:**
Educating and reframing community pharmacy's shift from a dispensing-led model toward a service-led clinical and private care model supported by Titan PMR and Titanverse.

**Key Assets:**
- Blog post
- Single image (Service-Led Health Hub graphic)
- LinkedIn post

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Positioning campaign:** Effectively reframed pharmacy business model shift from dispensing-led to service-led
- **Blog + image combination:** Blog provided depth, image provided visual summary
- **Moderate engagement:** 5.5% engagement rate and 3.5% CTR indicate interest in positioning content
- **Blog link drove clicks:** 54 clicks suggest blog link generated interest
- **Valuable comments:** Comments from Hooman Ghalamkari and Michael Holden validate positioning and provide strategic guidance
- **Reposts indicate interest:** 6 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Narrative context:** Campaign provided narrative context for Titanverse launch

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 2 comments despite 84 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Moderate CTR:** 3.5% CTR is moderate and could be improved with stronger CTA
- **Limited page views:** Only 6 page views suggests content didn't drive significant website traffic
- **No follower growth:** Only 1 follower gained suggests content didn't attract significant new audience

### Audience Insights

- **Positioning resonates:** Moderate engagement (5.5% engagement rate) indicates audience values positioning content
- **Blog format valuable:** Blog link drove clicks (54 clicks) suggesting audience values in-depth content
- **Visual summary effective:** Single image provided effective visual summary of blog content
- **Comments validate positioning:** Hooman Ghalamkari and Michael Holden comments validate positioning and provide strategic guidance
- **Reposts indicate shareability:** 6 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with colleagues

## Campaign Context

Positioning campaign effectively reframed pharmacy business model shift. Moderate engagement (5.5% engagement rate, 3.5% CTR) indicates interest in positioning content. **Significance:**
This campaign successfully positioned and reframed community pharmacy's shift from dispensing-led to service-led model. The moderate engagement (5.5% engagement rate, 3.5% CTR) indicates interest in positioning content, and the blog + image combination effectively provided both depth and visual summary. Comments from Hooman Ghalamkari and Michael Holden validate the positioning and provide strategic guidance, particularly Hooman's clear pathway (Titan PMR → Titanverse). The campaign successfully provides narrative context for Titanverse launch and frames private services as patient-driven rather than opportunistic. Future campaigns should leverage this success by creating more positioning content and emphasizing the strategic pathway from Titan PMR to Titanverse.
