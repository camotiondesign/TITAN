# From Dispensing to Health Hub

Positioning and reframing campaign educating and reframing community pharmacy's shift from a dispensing-led model toward a service-led clinical and private care model supported by Titan PMR and Titanverse.

## Primary Assets

- Blog post
- Single image (Service-Led Health Hub graphic)

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post

## Strategic Note

Positioning campaign effectively reframed pharmacy business model shift. Moderate engagement (5.5% engagement rate, 3.5% CTR) indicates interest in positioning content. Comments from Hooman Ghalamkari and Michael Holden validate positioning and provide strategic guidance. Blog link drove clicks (54 clicks). Reposts (6) indicate content was valuable for sharing. Campaign provides narrative context for Titanverse launch and frames private services as patient-driven.

---

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** December 4-5, 2025  
**Product:** Titanverse / Titan PMR  
**Type:** Positioning / Thought Leadership  
**Purpose:** Educating and reframing community pharmacy's shift from a dispensing-led model toward a service-led clinical and private care model supported by Titan PMR and Titanverse.

## Core Message

Dispensing alone is no longer sustainable. The shift to service-led health hub is happening. Titan PMR creates the capacity, Titanverse converts it into clinical and commercial value.
