## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Execution-focused messaging:** "Not just advice. Real execution" effectively differentiated AI Agents from advice-only tools
- **Feature showcase:** Visual effectively demonstrated Quick Actions capability (prescription generated, sent to pharmacy, emails sent)
- **Good engagement:** 8.3% engagement rate and 4.8% CTR indicate strong interest in AI automation features
- **Reposts indicate interest:** 5 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Page views:** 4 page viewers suggest content drove website interest
- **Visual design:** Dark Titanverse-branded poster with glowing purple interface effectively communicated product feature

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 64 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **No follower growth:** Zero followers gained suggests content didn't attract new audience
- **Moderate reach:** 770 impressions is moderate compared to other campaigns

### Audience Insights

- **AI automation resonates:** Good engagement (8.3% engagement rate) indicates audience values AI automation features
- **Execution differentiation valuable:** "Not just advice. Real execution" messaging effectively differentiated AI Agents
- **Quick Actions showcase works:** Visual demonstration of Quick Actions (prescription generated, sent to pharmacy, emails sent) resonated with audience
- **Reposts indicate shareability:** 5 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with colleagues
- **Website interest:** 4 page viewers suggest content drove some website traffic
