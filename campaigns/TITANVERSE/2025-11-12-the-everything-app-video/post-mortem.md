# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** The Everything App Video  
**Date Range:** 2025-11-12 to 2025-11-24  
**Product:** TITANVERSE  
**Campaign Type:** Product Positioning

**Objective:**
Position Titanverse as "The Everything App for Pharmacy Services" through full animated explainer video, showcasing comprehensive platform capabilities including smart consultations, AI templates, ambient voice technology, AI agents, and structured summaries.

- Full animated explainer video

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Comprehensive platform positioning:** "The Everything App for Pharmacy Services" effectively communicated unified platform value proposition
- **Good organic performance:** 8.9% organic engagement rate and 5.7% CTR indicate strong interest in unified platform solution
- **Boosted post increased reach:** 3,642 sponsored impressions significantly expanded audience
- **Video performance:** 493 views with 12-second average watch time demonstrates engaging animated format
- **Valuable comment:** Sunil Chandarana's comment ("Game changer") validates positioning and generates social proof
- **Feature showcase:** Video effectively demonstrated comprehensive platform capabilities

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited sponsored engagement:** 10 sponsored engagements from 3,642 impressions (0.3% engagement rate) suggests sponsored audience was significantly less engaged
- **Low sponsored CTR:** 6 clicks from 3,642 sponsored impressions (0.2% CTR) indicates sponsored content didn't drive clicks effectively
- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 100 total engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Limited page views:** Only 2 page viewers suggests content didn't drive significant website traffic

### Audience Insights

- **Unified platform resonates:** Good organic performance (8.9% engagement rate) indicates audience values unified platform solution
- **Boosted reach effective:** 3,642 sponsored impressions significantly expanded audience
- **Sponsored audience less engaged:** Low sponsored engagement rate (0.3%) suggests sponsored audience was significantly less interested than organic audience
- **Video format effective:** 12-second average watch time demonstrates engaging animated format
- **Positioning validated:** Sunil Chandarana's comment validates "Everything App" positioning

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue comprehensive positioning:** "The Everything App" messaging effectively communicated unified platform value proposition
- **Optimize sponsored targeting:** Low sponsored engagement suggests need for better audience targeting
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Create video series:** Build series of animated explainer videos for different platform aspects
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose video into carousel with feature breakdown

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Comprehensive platform positioning, feature showcase, animated format, clear messaging
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, use case examples, stronger CTA, video series
- **Test:** Different positioning angles, multiple platform focuses, carousel format

### Process Improvements

- **Product positioning workflow:** Develop process for creating product positioning video content
- **Animated video library:** Build library of animated explainer content for future use
- **Sponsored targeting strategy:** Develop strategy for optimizing sponsored audience targeting
- **Video series planning:** Develop strategy for animated explainer video series
