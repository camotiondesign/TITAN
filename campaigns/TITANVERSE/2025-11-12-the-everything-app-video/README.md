# The Everything App Video

Product positioning full animated explainer video positioning Titanverse as "The Everything App for Pharmacy Services". Comprehensive platform showcase demonstrating smart consultations, AI templates, ambient voice technology, AI agents, and structured summaries.

## Primary Asset

- Full animated explainer video

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post (boosted Nov 17-24)

## Strategic Note

Product positioning video effectively communicated comprehensive platform value proposition. Good organic performance (8.9% engagement rate, 5.7% CTR) indicates strong interest in unified platform solution. Comment from Sunil Chandarana ("Game changer") validates positioning and generates social proof. Sponsored performance significantly lower (0.3% engagement rate, 0.2% CTR) suggests need for better audience targeting. Video performance (493 views, 12-second average watch time) demonstrates engaging animated format.

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** November 12, 2025  
**Product:** Titanverse  
**Type:** Product Positioning  
**Purpose:** Position Titanverse as "The Everything App for Pharmacy Services" through full animated explainer video, showcasing comprehensive platform capabilities including smart consultations, AI templates, ambient voice technology, AI agents, and structured summaries.

## Core Message

The pharmacy doesn't need more tools. It needs one intelligent platform that does it all. Titanverse brings consultations, prescribing, payments, compliance and CRM together on one intelligent console.
