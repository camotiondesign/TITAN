# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** Titanverse Time for Services  
**Date Range:** 2025-10-29  
**Product:** TITANVERSE  
**Campaign Type:** Product Positioning

**Objective:**
Position Titanverse as the natural evolution from Titan PMR, transitioning from dispensing freedom to comprehensive pharmacy services platform through cosmic-themed visual narrative.

**Key Assets:**
- Single image (informative poster / feature showcase)
- LinkedIn post

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Product positioning narrative:** "Titan freed you from dispensing. Now it's time for services" effectively communicated evolution story
- **Cosmic-themed visual design:** Cosmic design successfully differentiated Titanverse brand and created memorable visual identity
- **Good engagement:** 8.1% engagement rate and 5.9% CTR indicate strong interest in services platform positioning
- **Strong follower growth:** 9 followers gained indicates content attracted new audience
- **Reposts indicate interest:** 6 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Feature showcase:** Visual feature tiles effectively communicated platform capabilities

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Zero comments despite 164 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **Moderate CTR:** 5.9% CTR is good but could be improved with stronger CTA
- **Limited page views:** Only 10 page viewers suggests content didn't drive significant website traffic

### Audience Insights

- **Evolution narrative resonates:** Good engagement (8.1% engagement rate) indicates audience relates to Titan → Titanverse evolution story
- **Services positioning valuable:** 5.9% CTR suggests strong interest in services platform positioning
- **Cosmic branding effective:** Cosmic-themed visual design successfully differentiated Titanverse brand
- **Feature showcase works:** Visual feature tiles effectively communicated platform capabilities
- **Strong follower growth:** 9 followers gained indicates content successfully attracted new audience
