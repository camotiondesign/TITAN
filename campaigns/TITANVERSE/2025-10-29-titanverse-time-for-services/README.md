# Titanverse Time for Services

Product positioning single image poster showing transition from Titan PMR dispensing products to Titanverse service features. Positions Titanverse as the natural evolution from dispensing freedom to comprehensive pharmacy services platform.

## Primary Asset

- Single image (informative poster / feature showcase)

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post

## Strategic Note

Product positioning image effectively communicated Titan → Titanverse evolution narrative. Good engagement (8.1% engagement rate, 5.9% CTR) indicates strong interest in services platform positioning. Strong follower growth (9 followers gained) and reposts (6) indicate content attracted new audience and was valuable for sharing. Cosmic-themed visual design successfully differentiated Titanverse brand.
