# Titanverse One App One Flow

Product positioning video featuring animated loop showing fragmented current workflow transitioning to Titanverse unified system. Positions Titanverse as the solution to pharmacy service fragmentation.

## Primary Asset

## Supporting Assets

## Strategic Note

Product positioning video effectively communicated unified platform value proposition. Good engagement (8.1% engagement rate, 5.7% CTR) indicates strong interest in fragmentation solution.
