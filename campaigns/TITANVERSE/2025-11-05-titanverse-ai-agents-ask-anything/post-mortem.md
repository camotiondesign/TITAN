# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** Titanverse AI Agents Ask Anything  
**Date Range:** 2025-11-05  
**Product:** TITANVERSE

**Objective:**

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Relatable consultation questions:** "What's next?", "Should I refer?", "Did I miss anything?" effectively captured pharmacist concerns
- **AI feature showcase:** Static graphic effectively communicated AI Agents capability
- **Good engagement:** 5.6% engagement rate and 3.5% CTR indicate strong interest in AI-powered clinical support
- **High reposts:** 8 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Valuable comment:** Hooman Ghalamkari's comment reinforces evidence-based clinical templates messaging and validates importance of structured clinical support
- **Training emphasis:** "Trained by real pharmacists on real clinical cases" effectively communicated credibility

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 64 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Limited follower growth:** Only 1 follower gained suggests content didn't attract significant new audience
- **Moderate CTR:** 3.5% CTR is moderate and could be improved with stronger CTA

### Audience Insights

- **AI clinical support resonates:** Good engagement (5.6% engagement rate) indicates audience values AI-powered clinical support
- **Evidence-based important:** Hooman Ghalamkari's comment highlights importance of evidence-based clinical templates
- **Structured support valuable:** Comment validates importance of structured clinical support for complex conditions
- **Relatable questions work:** Consultation questions ("What's next?", "Should I refer?", "Did I miss anything?") resonated with pharmacists
- **High shareability:** 8 reposts indicate content was valuable for sharing with colleagues

## Recommendations

- **Continue AI feature focus:** AI Agents feature effectively communicated value proposition
- **Emphasize evidence-based:** Continue highlighting evidence-based clinical templates and structured support
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Create AI series:** Build series of AI feature-focused content

- **Keep:** Relatable consultation questions, AI feature showcase, evidence-based messaging, training emphasis

- **Test:** Different AI feature angles, multiple consultation scenarios, carousel format

- **AI feature workflow:** Develop process for creating AI feature-focused content
