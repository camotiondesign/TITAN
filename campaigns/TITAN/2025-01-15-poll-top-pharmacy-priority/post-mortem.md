## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Direct audience insight that validated strategic direction:** Poll results confirmed private services as top priority
- **High click rate relative to impressions:** 4.0% CTR strong for poll format
- **Solid engagement for a poll without incentives:** 22 votes and 45 engagements without promotional incentives
- **Strategic validation:** Results support Titan's positioning for service-led workflow design

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited reach due to inherent poll distribution limits:** 1,112 impressions lower than other content types
- **No comments generated:** Despite asking for priorities, no discussion in comments
- **Algorithmic throttling:** Polls receive less distribution compared to carousels and videos

### Audience Insights

- **Private services dominant:** 73% of votes for expanding private services validates growth narrative
- **Workflow efficiency secondary:** Only 14% prioritised workflow efficiency, suggesting it's assumed/expected
- **Service-led focus:** Results support Titanverse positioning and service-led workflow messaging
- **Revenue focus:** Clear signal that private services are becoming the revenue focus

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Set up a two-part poll (service: which type):** Follow-up poll to dive deeper into specific service types
- **Follow poll with carousel summarising results + implications:** Create carousel highlighting poll results and what they mean
- **Use poll outcome in sales scripts:** Incorporate poll insights into sales and marketing messaging
- **Test poll variants:** Try different question angles to gather more specific insights

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Direct audience insight gathering, strategic validation approach, poll format
- **Add:** Follow-up content summarising results, carousel with implications, sales script integration
- **Test:** Two-part polls, result summarisation formats, discussion-driving prompts

### Process Improvements

- **Poll result integration:** Develop process for incorporating poll insights into messaging and positioning
- **Follow-up content workflow:** Create templates for summarising poll results and implications
- **Sales script updates:** Establish process for updating sales scripts with poll insights
- **Multi-part poll strategy:** Develop strategy for sequential polls that build on each other
