# Poll — Top Pharmacy Priority 2025

A LinkedIn poll designed to gather audience sentiment on the industry's focus for the year. Results overwhelmingly highlighted private service expansion as the leading priority, outpacing workflow efficiency, error reduction, and patient care. This insight supports Titan's positioning for service-led workflow design and future Titanverse content.

## Primary Asset

## Supporting Assets

## Outcome

Clear signal—private services are becoming the revenue focus.

## Strategic Note

This should influence messaging toward service enablement, workflow freeing, and private-clinic economics.
