## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Product demonstration worked:** Real pharmacy team demonstration effectively communicated workflow features (batch flow, repeat flow, paperless audit trail)
- **Good engagement:** 7.8% engagement rate and 5.8% CTR indicate strong audience interest in product demonstrations
- **Strong watch time:** 18-second average watch time suggests content held attention and demonstrated value
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with peers
- **Workflow messaging:** "Pharmacists step back. Dispensers step up." messaging resonated with audience
- **Feature demonstration:** Content effectively demonstrated batch flow, repeat flow, and paperless audit trail features

### What Didn't Work

- **No comments:** Zero comments despite 85 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs (time saved, efficiency gains, etc.)

### Audience Insights

- **Product demonstrations resonate:** Good engagement (7.8%) indicates strong interest in product demonstration content
- **Workflow messaging works:** "Pharmacists step back. Dispensers step up." messaging resonated with audience
- **Watch time indicates value:** 18-second average watch time suggests content was valuable and demonstrated features effectively
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Feature demonstration effective:** Content effectively demonstrated workflow features

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue product demonstrations:** Product demonstrations consistently resonate with audience
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Add quantified benefits:** Include specific KPIs (time saved, efficiency gains, etc.)
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose demonstration into carousel with feature breakdown
- **Create demonstration series:** Build series of demonstrations for different features

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Product demonstration format, workflow messaging, feature demonstration, real team members
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, quantified benefits, feature breakdown, demonstration series
- **Test:** Carousel format, multiple feature demonstrations, testimonial integration

### Process Improvements

- **Demonstration workflow:** Develop process for creating product demonstration videos
- **Feature demonstration library:** Build library of feature demonstrations for future content
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Product demonstration template:** Create template for product demonstration content
