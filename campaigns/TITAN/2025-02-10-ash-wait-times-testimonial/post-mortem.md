## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Waiting times messaging:** Testimonial effectively communicated reduced waiting times benefit, addressing core patient concern
- **Team member perspective:** Testimonial from pharmacy team member (not owner) adds authenticity and relatability
- **Efficiency improvements:** Content highlighted easier prescription finding and smoother processing
- **Good engagement:** 7.6% engagement rate and 5.9% CTR indicate strong audience interest in efficiency improvements
- **Strong watch time:** 11-second average watch time suggests content held attention
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with peers
- **Positive feedback:** Comments from Arlene Osman, Ishani D., and Tean Thomas validate message and approach
- **Follower growth:** 3 followers gained indicates content attracted new audience

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 3 comments despite 80 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs (waiting time reduction, time saved, etc.)

### Audience Insights

- **Waiting times resonate:** High engagement (7.6%) indicates strong demand for waiting time reduction solutions
- **Team member perspective works:** Testimonial from pharmacy team member adds authenticity and relatability
- **Watch time indicates value:** 11-second average watch time suggests content was valuable
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Efficiency messaging works:** Content highlighting easier prescription finding and smoother processing resonated

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Add quantified benefits:** Include specific KPIs (waiting time reduction, time saved, prescriptions processed faster, etc.)
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose testimonial into carousel with specific benefits and outcomes
- **Include before/after comparison:** Add visual/text comparison showing waiting time transformation
- **Create team member testimonial series:** Build series of testimonials from different team member roles

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Waiting times messaging, team member perspective, efficiency improvements, testimonial format
- **Add:** Quantified KPIs, discussion prompts, before/after comparisons, team member testimonial series
- **Test:** Carousel format, multiple statistic variants, testimonial integration

### Process Improvements

- **KPI extraction workflow:** Develop process for identifying and quantifying waiting time and efficiency benefits
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Carousel repurposing:** Develop workflow for converting testimonials into carousel format
- **Team member testimonial library:** Build library of team member testimonials for future content
