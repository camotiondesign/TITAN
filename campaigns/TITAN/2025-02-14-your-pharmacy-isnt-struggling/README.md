# Your Pharmacy Isn't Struggling

Carousel campaign reframing pharmacy challenges by positioning legacy PMRs as the problem, not pharmacies themselves. Exceptional performance demonstrates strong resonance with audience frustration.

## Primary Asset

- LinkedIn carousel (7 slides)

## Supporting Assets

## Strategic Note

Reframing messaging resonated strongly with audience frustration. Exceptional engagement (53.4% engagement rate, 51.8% CTR) demonstrates high interest in content addressing legacy PMR pain points.

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** February 14, 2025  
**Product:** Titan PMR  
**Type:** Brand Positioning  
**Purpose:** Reframe pharmacy challenges by positioning legacy PMRs as the problem, not pharmacies themselves, and position TITAN as the solution.

## Core Message

Your pharmacy isn't struggling—your PMR is. Legacy systems create bottlenecks, slow workflows, and limit growth.
