## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Reframing messaging resonated:** Content successfully reframed pharmacy challenges by positioning legacy PMRs as the problem, not pharmacies themselves
- **Exceptional engagement:** 53.4% engagement rate and 51.8% CTR demonstrate extremely high audience interest
- **Discussion prompt worked:** Prompt ("What's the biggest tech frustration slowing your pharmacy down?") encouraged comments
- **Strong testimonial:** Comment from Owen Keil ("Everyone is always shocked to see no baskets, no queues, no mania. Stress free staff.") validated message and provided social proof
- **Pain point identification:** Content effectively identified key pain points (slow workflows, manual admin, no barcode validation, lack of visibility)
- **Reposts indicate value:** 5 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with peers
- **Empowerment messaging:** "Pharmacists aren't the problem. Outdated tech is." messaging empowered audience

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 4 comments despite 1,349 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs or measurable outcomes from TITAN
- **No direct solution callout:** Content focused on problems but didn't explicitly call out TITAN as solution until end

### Audience Insights

- **Reframing messaging highly effective:** Exceptional engagement (53.4%) indicates strong demand for content reframing pharmacy challenges
- **Legacy PMR frustration resonates:** Content addressing legacy PMR pain points resonated strongly with audience
- **Click-through indicates value:** High CTR (51.8%) suggests audience found content valuable and worth exploring
- **Empowerment messaging works:** "Pharmacists aren't the problem. Outdated tech is." messaging empowered audience
- **Pain point identification effective:** Content effectively identified key pain points that resonated with audience

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue reframing messaging:** Reframing approach consistently high-performing and resonates with audience frustration
- **Add quantified benefits:** Include specific KPIs or measurable outcomes from TITAN to support solution positioning
- **Explicit solution callout:** Include direct TITAN callout earlier in content to connect problems to solution
- **Expand testimonial use:** Leverage strong testimonials like Owen Keil's in carousel or follow-up content
- **Test discussion prompt variants:** Experiment with different discussion prompts to encourage broader engagement

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Reframing messaging, pain point identification, empowerment messaging, discussion prompts
- **Add:** Quantified benefits, explicit solution callout, testimonial integration, before/after comparisons
- **Test:** Different reframing angles, solution positioning, testimonial formats

### Process Improvements

- **Reframing message library:** Build library of reframing messages for pharmacy challenges
- **Pain point mapping:** Develop framework for identifying and addressing legacy PMR pain points
- **Testimonial extraction:** Develop process for extracting and leveraging strong testimonials
- **Discussion prompt library:** Build library of discussion prompts that encourage engagement
