# Why Pharmacies Are Switching to TITAN PMR (Trustpilot Reviews)

LinkedIn carousel campaign featuring Trustpilot reviews from real pharmacy owners. Social proof campaign highlighting why pharmacies are switching to TITAN PMR through authentic customer testimonials.

## Primary Asset

## Supporting Assets

## Strategic Note

Social proof through Trustpilot reviews builds trust and credibility. Real pharmacy owner testimonials validate product quality and address common concerns about switching PMR systems.
