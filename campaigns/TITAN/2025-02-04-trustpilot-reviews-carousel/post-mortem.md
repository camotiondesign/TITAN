## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Social proof through Trustpilot:** Real pharmacy owner reviews built trust and credibility effectively
- **Authentic testimonials:** Real customer testimonials validated product quality and addressed concerns
- **Exceptional engagement:** 48.4% engagement rate and 46.9% CTR demonstrate extremely high audience interest
- **Review format resonates:** Carousel format effectively showcased multiple testimonials
- **Trustpilot credibility:** Trustpilot platform added credibility and trust to reviews
- **Reposts indicate value:** 3 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with peers

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 639 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **Low reactions relative to clicks:** 16 reactions vs 619 clicks suggests content was informative but not emotionally engaging
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion

### Audience Insights

- **Social proof highly effective:** Exceptional engagement (48.4%) indicates strong demand for social proof content
- **Trustpilot reviews build trust:** Real pharmacy owner reviews from Trustpilot platform built credibility
- **Click-through indicates value:** High CTR (46.9%) suggests audience found content valuable and worth exploring
- **Review format works:** Carousel format effectively showcased multiple testimonials
- **Authentic testimonials resonate:** Real customer testimonials validated product quality and addressed concerns

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue Trustpilot reviews:** Social proof through Trustpilot reviews consistently high-performing
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Test review variants:** Experiment with different review selections or themes
- **Expand to other formats:** Repurpose carousel content into blog posts, videos, or other formats
- **Build review library:** Create library of Trustpilot reviews for future content

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Social proof format, Trustpilot reviews, authentic testimonials, carousel format
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, review themes, testimonial quotes, before/after comparisons
- **Test:** Different review selections, testimonial integration, discussion-driven formats

### Process Improvements

- **Review library:** Build library of Trustpilot reviews organized by theme or benefit
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Carousel template:** Create template for social proof carousels with proven structure
- **Review selection workflow:** Develop process for selecting and organizing reviews for campaigns

## Next Steps
