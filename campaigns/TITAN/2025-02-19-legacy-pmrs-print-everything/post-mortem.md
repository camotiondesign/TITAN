## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Visual format worked:** Animated loop effectively communicated legacy PMR pain points (excessive printing, chaos, outdated behavior)
- **Good engagement:** 8.9% engagement rate and 6.8% CTR indicate strong audience interest
- **Strong watch time:** 10-second average watch time suggests content held attention
- **Comments validated positioning:** Comments from Aron Garner ("Always developing, constantly improving and staying ahead") and Balal Anwar ("They call it the 'TITAN PMR' way") validated innovation positioning
- **Reposts indicate value:** 5 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with peers
- **Follower growth:** 1 follower gained indicates content attracted new audience
- **Contrast messaging:** "That's like celebrating fax machines in 2025" effectively communicated outdated nature of printing

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 4 comments despite 122 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs or measurable outcomes from digital approach

### Audience Insights

- **Visual format resonates:** Animated loop effectively communicated legacy PMR pain points
- **Contrast messaging works:** "That's like celebrating fax machines in 2025" resonated with audience
- **Watch time indicates value:** 10-second average watch time suggests content was valuable
- **Reposts indicate value:** 5 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Innovation positioning validated:** Comments validated TITAN as innovation leader

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue visual format:** Animated loops effectively communicate legacy PMR pain points
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Add quantified benefits:** Include specific KPIs or measurable outcomes from digital approach
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose animated content into carousel with more detail
- **Leverage innovation comments:** Use comments validating innovation positioning in future content

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Visual animation format, contrast messaging, innovation positioning, legacy PMR pain points
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, quantified benefits, innovation proof points, carousel variants
- **Test:** Different visual concepts, discussion prompt variations, innovation proof points

### Process Improvements

- **Visual animation library:** Build library of animated loops for legacy PMR pain points
- **Contrast messaging library:** Develop library of contrast messages (fax machines, etc.)
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Innovation proof point library:** Build library of innovation examples and proof points
