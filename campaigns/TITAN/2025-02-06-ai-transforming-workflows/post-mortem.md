## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Educational content resonates:** 7.5% engagement rate indicates strong interest in AI education
- **Clear feature-to-outcome logic:** Infographic effectively communicated AI benefits through clear structure
- **AI-first positioning:** Content positioned TITAN as AI-first without hype, building credibility
- **Visual format works:** Infographic format performed well for saves and revisits
- **Partnership interest:** Comments from ClinicRx suggest partnership opportunities and solution provider engagement
- **Strong reactions:** 32 reactions indicate positive sentiment and content value
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 2 comments despite 111 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs beyond "2+ hours saved daily"

### Audience Insights

- **Educational content performs:** 7.5% engagement rate indicates strong demand for AI education
- **Solution providers engaged:** Strong engagement from solution providers and partnership-minded stakeholders
- **Visual format resonates:** Infographic format performed well for saves and revisits
- **AI-first positioning works:** Content positioned TITAN as AI-first without hype, building credibility
- **Partnership opportunities:** Comments from ClinicRx suggest partnership opportunities
- **Feature-to-outcome logic effective:** Clear structure effectively communicated AI benefits

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue educational format:** Educational explainer content consistently high-performing
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Expand to carousel format:** Repurpose infographic into carousel with more detail per feature
- **Add quantified benefits:** Include specific KPIs and measurable outcomes
- **Leverage partnership interest:** Follow up on partnership opportunities from solution providers

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Educational format, clear feature-to-outcome logic, AI-first positioning, visual infographic format
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, quantified benefits, partnership messaging, carousel variants
- **Test:** Carousel format, testimonial integration, before/after comparisons

### Process Improvements

- **Educational content library:** Build library of educational infographics on AI and pharmacy topics
- **Partnership workflow:** Develop process for following up on partnership opportunities
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Infographic template:** Create template for AI education infographics with proven structure
