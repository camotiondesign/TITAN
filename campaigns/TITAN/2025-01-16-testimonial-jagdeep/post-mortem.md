## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Reinforces private services narrative:** Content successfully supports service-led transformation theme
- **Strong alignment with audience validation from poll:** Testimonial aligns with poll results showing expanding private services as priority
- **Clear emotional and operational benefit:** Time-saving and service expansion benefits clearly communicated
- **High engagement:** 7.5% engagement rate and 6.2% CTR demonstrate strong performance

### What Didn't Work

- **Could add example services offered now:** Missing specific service names (travel, weight management, etc.)
- **Limited specificity:** General "more services" messaging lacks concrete examples
- **No named services:** Specific service names would increase relatability

### Audience Insights

- **Service expansion interest:** High engagement suggests audience interest in service expansion messaging
- **Time-saving value:** Testimonial resonates with pharmacy owners seeking to free up time
- **Relatability opportunity:** Specific service names would increase connection with audience
- **Strategic validation:** Content validates poll insights about private services priority

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Include named services (travel, weight management, etc.):** Add specific service examples to increase relatability
- **Add on-screen text: "Unlocked: X new services":** Include visual text overlays showing specific service count
- **Create carousel follow-up:** Expand testimonial into multi-slide format with specific services and benefits
- **Service-specific variants:** Create variants highlighting different service types

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Time-saving messaging, service expansion theme, testimonial format, strategic alignment
- **Add:** Specific service names, service count, visual text overlays, carousel format
- **Test:** Service-specific variants, carousel follow-ups, on-screen text overlays

### Process Improvements

- **Service naming workflow:** Develop process for identifying and naming specific services in testimonials
- **Carousel follow-up templates:** Create templates for expanding testimonials into service-focused carousels
- **Strategic pairing:** Establish process for pairing testimonials with poll results and audience insights
- **Service library:** Build library of service examples for use in content
