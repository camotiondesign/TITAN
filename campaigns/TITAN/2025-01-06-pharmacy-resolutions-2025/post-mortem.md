## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Timely post-new-year timing:** Posted January 6, perfect timing for re-engagement after holidays
- **Strong alignment to AI narrative:** Successfully positioned Titan with AI-enabled transformation
- **Checklist visual makes value points skimmable:** Format allows quick consumption of key messages
- **Future-facing momentum:** Campaign aligns Titan with forward-looking pharmacy transformation

### What Didn't Work

- **No comments — engagement was passive:** 0 comments suggests audience consumed but didn't engage in conversation
- **Could have been more provocative or specific:** Vision-positive but lacks challenge/solution framing
- **Limited conversation:** No discussion despite asking for resolutions in caption

### Audience Insights

- **Passive consumption:** High engagement rate but no comments suggests visual consumption without discussion
- **Vision-positive messaging:** Content resonated but didn't drive active participation
- **AI narrative alignment:** Strong positioning for AI-enabled transformation messaging

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Add poll asking: which resolution matters most?:** Create interactive element to drive engagement
- **"This resolution won't work without…" angle:** Add challenge/solution framing for more provocative content
- **Carousel follow-up on how Titan delivers each tick:** Expand checklist into multi-slide format with specific Titan features
- **More specific provocations:** Include specific challenges or pain points to drive discussion

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Timely timing, AI narrative alignment, checklist format, future-facing momentum
- **Add:** Interactive elements (polls, questions), challenge/solution framing, specific Titan features
- **Test:** Carousel format, video format, more provocative messaging

### Process Improvements

- **Reference in future content:** Use this post as reference point for AI workloads, automation benefits, paperless pharmacy concepts
- **Follow-up content strategy:** Develop carousel or video series expanding on each resolution
- **Engagement tactics:** Include more direct questions or interactive elements to drive conversation
