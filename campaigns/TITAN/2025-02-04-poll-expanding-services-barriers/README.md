# Poll — What's Holding Pharmacies Back From Expanding Services?

LinkedIn poll campaign gathering market insights on barriers preventing pharmacies from expanding services. Market insight campaign revealing primary barriers and informing messaging strategy.

## Primary Asset

- LinkedIn poll

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post

## Strategic Note

Poll results reveal time & staffing shortages (47%) as the primary barrier, validating messaging around time-saving and efficiency benefits. This insight influences future content strategy toward service enablement messaging.
