## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Market insight gathered:** Poll successfully revealed time & staffing shortages (47%) as primary barrier to service expansion
- **Messaging validation:** Results validate messaging around time-saving and efficiency benefits
- **Strategic value:** Insight influences future content strategy toward service enablement messaging
- **Barrier identification:** Poll identified key barriers: time & staffing (47%), regulations (27%), lack of demand (20%), cost (7%)
- **Context provided:** Caption referenced previous poll showing 73% prioritize expanding services, creating context

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited participation:** Only 15 votes despite 1,105 impressions suggests low engagement with poll format
- **No comments:** Zero comments despite 42 engagements suggests poll didn't spark discussion
- **Low engagement rate:** 3.8% engagement rate lower than other content formats
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion

### Audience Insights

- **Time & staffing is primary barrier:** 47% of respondents identified time & staffing shortages as main barrier
- **Regulations secondary concern:** 27% identified regulations & compliance as barrier
- **Demand uncertainty exists:** 20% identified lack of demand or uncertainty as barrier
- **Cost less of a concern:** Only 7% identified cost & financial risk as barrier
- **Poll format engagement:** Lower engagement rate (3.8%) compared to other formats suggests polls may need more promotion

- **Create results carousel:** Repurpose poll results into carousel format for broader reach
