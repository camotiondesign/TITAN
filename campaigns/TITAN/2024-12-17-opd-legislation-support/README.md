# Original Pack Dispensing (OPD) — Titan PMR Support

This campaign announced Titan's readiness for the January 2025 OPD regulatory change. 

It included a detailed article authored by CTO Wahid Muhammad and a supporting 

LinkedIn single-image post.

## Purpose

Reassure pharmacy owners that Titan handles OPD seamlessly and automatically within 

existing workflows, reinforcing Titan's positioning as a compliant, future-ready 

platform.

## Primary Asset

- Article explaining OPD and Titan's automation delivery

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn single-image announcement

## Outcome

Moderate engagement with strong shareability, as indicated by reposts. Future content 

should include animation or visual workflow demonstration to enhance clarity and impact.

---

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** December 17, 2024  
**Product:** Titan PMR  
**Type:** Regulation Update  
**Purpose:** Announce Titan PMR's readiness for the new Original Pack Dispensing legislation and reassure pharmacies that compliance will be automatic and seamless.

## Core Message

OPD becomes mandatory in January 2025 and Titan PMR already supports it through automation and barcode-driven accuracy.
