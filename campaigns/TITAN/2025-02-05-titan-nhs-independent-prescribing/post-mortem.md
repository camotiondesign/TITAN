## Learnings

### What Worked

- **NHS alignment messaging:** Content successfully demonstrated NHS alignment and innovation leadership
- **High signal asset:** NHS alignment + future-facing innovation resonated strongly with audience
- **Social proof from NHS leadership:** Comments from NHS stakeholders (Ghulam Haydar, Wasim Baqir) boosted confidence and credibility
- **Exceptional engagement:** 63.7% engagement rate and 62.4% CTR demonstrate extremely high audience interest
- **Broader audience engagement:** Strong engagement from policy / tech stakeholders vs typical pharmacist-only audience
- **Future-facing positioning:** Independent prescribing messaging positioned TITAN as innovation leader
- **CLEO compatibility:** Clear explanation of CLEO compatibility addressed potential concerns

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 3 comments despite 3,465 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Sponsored performance lower:** 17.7% sponsored engagement rate lower than organic (63.7%)

### Audience Insights

- **NHS alignment highly valued:** Exceptional engagement (63.7%) indicates strong demand for NHS alignment messaging
- **Policy / tech stakeholders engaged:** Broader audience engagement suggests content resonated beyond typical pharmacist audience
- **Social proof from NHS leadership:** Comments from NHS stakeholders boosted confidence and credibility
- **Future-facing innovation resonates:** Independent prescribing messaging positioned TITAN as innovation leader
- **Click-through indicates value:** High CTR (62.4%) suggests audience found content valuable and worth exploring

- **Continue NHS alignment messaging:** NHS alignment content consistently high-performing
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Leverage NHS stakeholder comments:** Use NHS stakeholder comments as social proof in future content
- **Expand to other formats:** Repurpose carousel content into blog posts, videos, or other formats
- **Build NHS alignment library:** Create library of NHS alignment content and messaging

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** NHS alignment messaging, future-facing innovation positioning, CLEO compatibility explanation, social proof
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, NHS stakeholder quotes, follow-up content, results carousel
- **Test:** Different NHS alignment angles, stakeholder testimonials, policy-focused formats

### Process Improvements

- **NHS alignment library:** Build library of NHS alignment content and messaging
- **Stakeholder engagement:** Develop process for engaging NHS stakeholders in content
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Policy content template:** Create template for policy-focused content with proven structure
