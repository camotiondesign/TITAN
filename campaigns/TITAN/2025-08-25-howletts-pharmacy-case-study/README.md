# 2025-08-25 Howletts Pharmacy Case Study

**Case Study: Howletts Pharmacy: Calm, Digital, and Service-Led**

Howletts Pharmacy case study featuring Pritee's transformation of a paper-heavy pharmacy into a digital-first, service-led operation that prioritizes calm workflows, team empowerment, and community connection.

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** August 25, 2025  
**Product:** Titan PMR  
**Type:** Case Study  
**Subject:** Howletts Pharmacy (Pritee)

## Key Metrics

- 6,500+ items dispensed monthly
- 50-60% of prescriptions clinically checked by Titan AI
- 20-30% dispensing time saved with Batch Flow
- 10+ hours a week saved on pharmacist accuracy checks
- 60-70 blood pressure checks a month (up from 10-15)
- From 1 private consultation a month → 2-3 every day
- End-of-month claims done in minutes not days
