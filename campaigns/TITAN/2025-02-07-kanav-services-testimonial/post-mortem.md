## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Service expansion messaging:** Testimonial effectively communicated how TITAN enables multiple services (weight management, travel health, Pharmacy First, earwax removal)
- **Since day one positioning:** "Since day one" messaging emphasized early adoption and foundation, building credibility
- **Good engagement:** 8.0% engagement rate and 6.3% CTR indicate strong audience interest in service expansion
- **Strong watch time:** 13-second average watch time suggests content held attention
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with peers
- **Positive feedback:** Comments from Balal Anwar and AlphaRize validate message and approach
- **Multiple services mentioned:** Specific service examples (weight management, travel health, Pharmacy First, earwax removal) increased relatability

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 2 comments despite 120 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs (revenue generated, services added, time saved, etc.)

### Audience Insights

- **Service expansion interest:** High engagement (8.0%) indicates strong demand for service expansion solutions
- **Since day one messaging works:** Early adoption messaging builds credibility and trust
- **Watch time indicates value:** 13-second average watch time suggests content was valuable
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Multiple services resonate:** Specific service examples increased relatability and connection

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Add quantified benefits:** Include specific KPIs (revenue generated, services added, time saved, etc.)
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose testimonial into carousel with specific services and benefits
- **Include before/after comparison:** Add visual/text comparison showing transformation
- **Create service-specific variants:** Create variants highlighting different service types

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Service expansion messaging, since day one positioning, multiple services mentioned, testimonial format
- **Add:** Quantified KPIs, discussion prompts, before/after comparisons, service-specific variants
- **Test:** Carousel format, multiple statistic variants, testimonial integration

### Process Improvements

- **KPI extraction workflow:** Develop process for identifying and quantifying service expansion benefits
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Carousel repurposing:** Develop workflow for converting testimonials into carousel format
- **Service library:** Build library of service examples for use in content
