## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Strong scaling message:** "50 patients at a time" statistic captured attention and communicated value
- **Efficiency gains clear:** Picking all 50 patients' medication in one go resonated with audience
- **Scaling without increasing man hours:** Key benefit communicated effectively and addressed core concern
- **Good engagement:** 8.5% engagement rate and 7.4% CTR indicate strong audience interest
- **Strong watch time:** 15-second average watch time suggests content held attention
- **Positive feedback:** Comment from AlphaRize ("Inspiring") validates message and approach

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 117 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs (time saved, efficiency gains, etc.)

### Audience Insights

- **Scaling capabilities interest:** High engagement (8.5%) indicates strong demand for scaling solutions
- **Efficiency messaging resonates:** Content addressing efficiency gains and time savings resonated
- **Watch time indicates value:** 15-second average watch time suggests content was valuable
- **Reposts indicate value:** 2 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing
- **Scaling without increasing costs:** Message about scaling without increasing man hours resonated strongly

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Add quantified benefits:** Include specific KPIs (time saved, efficiency gains, cost savings, etc.)
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Test carousel format:** Repurpose testimonial into carousel with statistics and benefits
- **Include before/after comparison:** Add visual/text comparison showing transformation
- **Create clip variants:** Extract key moments for short-form content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Scaling messaging, efficiency gains, testimonial format, clear benefits
- **Add:** Quantified KPIs, discussion prompts, before/after comparisons, clip variants
- **Test:** Carousel format, multiple statistic variants, testimonial integration

### Process Improvements

- **KPI extraction workflow:** Develop process for identifying and quantifying scaling benefits
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Carousel repurposing:** Develop workflow for converting testimonials into carousel format
- **Scaling stat library:** Build library of scaling statistics and proof points for future content

## Next Steps

## Campaign Context

**Key Insight:**
Strong scaling message ("50 patients at a time") and efficiency gains resonated with audience. Scaling without increasing man hours addressed core concern.

**Significance:**
This campaign successfully demonstrated scaling capabilities through real pharmacist testimonial. The "50 patients at a time" statistic and efficiency gains (picking all 50 patients' medication in one go) resonated strongly with the audience. Good engagement (8.5% engagement rate, 7.4% CTR) and strong watch time (15 seconds average) indicate strong audience interest in scaling solutions. The message about scaling without increasing man hours addressed a core concern for pharmacy owners. Future campaigns should include quantified KPIs and discussion prompts to enhance impact.
