# 2025-11-10 BMP Healthcare Case Study

**Case Study: How BMP Scaled to 170,000 Items and 500 Percent Service Growth**

BMP Healthcare case study featuring Yusuf's transformation of a 12-branch independent group across the Midlands, achieving 70% reduction in clinical checking, 2x faster dispensing, and 500% service growth through Titan PMR and Titanverse.

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** November 10, 2025  
**Product:** Titan PMR + Titanverse  
**Type:** Case Study  
**Subject:** BMP Healthcare, Midlands (Yusuf)

## Key Metrics

- 170,000 items dispensed monthly across 12 branches
- 70% reduction in clinical checking (down to 20-30%)
- 2× faster dispensing speed with Batch Flow
- 500% increase in clinical services
- Human error eliminated through barcode scanning
- Staff retrained and upskilled, not reduced
