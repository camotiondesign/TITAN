## B. Fresh Angles This Interview Adds

### 1. Near Miss Reduction (CRITICAL GAP)
- **Unique:** 5-6 near misses/month → 2 in a year
- **Quote:** "We would have between five to six near misses a month easily on proscript. I've had two since we've had Titan, and, you know, that's in a year"
- **Why it matters:** Safety angle not deeply covered elsewhere. Inspector skepticism story adds credibility.

### 2. Single Patient Mode for Small Pharmacies
- **Unique:** Space-constrained workflow solution
- **Quote:** "Single patient mode, which is great for patient people who don't have the space to line up 20 baskets"
- **Why it matters:** Addresses small pharmacy constraint not covered in other case studies (most focus on high-volume).

### 3. Staff Empowerment & Responsibility Shift
- **Unique:** Staff "have a patient, not just a number"
- **Quote:** "They have a patient. This patient's medication hasn't come in. I need to chase that supplier"
- **Why it matters:** Goes beyond upskilling to ownership and meaningful work narrative.

### 4. Generational Adoption Story
- **Unique:** 80-year-old superintendent using Titan
- **Quote:** "Seifie himself, ripe old, age of 80. Is still the superintendent still going strong... He gets on, he just gets on, and he loves it"
- **Why it matters:** Addresses "too old to learn new tech" objection.

### 5. Competitive Market Dynamics
- **Unique:** Patient expectations in high-competition area
- **Quote:** "They won't pay an Amazon Prime subscription, but they want it on the next day. And unfortunately, if you don't pander to their demands, well, they'll go down the road to another pharmacy"
- **Why it matters:** Real-world competitive pressure context.

### 6. Repeat Flow Deep Dive
- **Unique:** Detailed workflow explanation
- **Quote:** "Repeat flow came massively in use... bulk order... cycles... Pharmapi integration"
- **Why it matters:** Repeat Flow mentioned elsewhere but not explained in detail.

### 7. Rapid Staff Onboarding
- **Unique:** 4 new staff trained in a week
- **Quote:** "I've just had four new stuff start. They've been here a week. They've taken it like a duck to water"
- **Why it matters:** Ease of use proof point.

### 8. Patient Experience Improvements
- **Unique:** Text messaging, barcode scanning for collections, shelf auditing
- **Quote:** "They will get text messages every time the medicine is ready... scan the barcode... never been a case so far in a year touch wood that a patient's prescription has not been found"
- **Why it matters:** Patient-facing benefits not deeply covered elsewhere.

### 9. Cost Savings (Tangible)
- **Unique:** Toner, paper, waste reduction quantified
- **Quote:** "We'd order three or four toners a month... confidential waste bins don't get full up as quick"
- **Why it matters:** ROI angle beyond time savings.

### 10. Titan AI Evolution Story
- **Unique:** Beta testing, version comparison, learning models
- **Quote:** "Titan X would check around 30 to 40%... new Titan AI is going to be checking 80 to 90%... I've been using it for about six months now"
- **Why it matters:** Product development narrative, early adopter credibility.
