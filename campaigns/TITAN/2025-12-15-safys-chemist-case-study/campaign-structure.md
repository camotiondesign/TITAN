# Optimal Campaign Structure: Safy's Chemist Case Study

**Campaign:** Asif's Safy's Chemist Case Study  
**Duration:** 4 weeks (with long-tail recycling)  
**Goal:** Maximum value extraction from one interview

---

## A. MANDATORY ASSETS

### 1. Written Case Study
- **Purpose:** SEO, credibility, detailed story
- **Audience:** Pharmacy owners researching solutions, decision-makers
- **Expected Outcome:** 200-500 reads, 5-10% conversion to demo requests
- **Best Timing:** Week 1, Day 1 (foundation asset)
- **File:** `content/blog/blog.md`

### 2. Longform Video (5-7 minutes)
- **Purpose:** Emotional connection, detailed narrative, YouTube/website hero
- **Audience:** Deep researchers, video-first audience
- **Expected Outcome:** 500-1,000 views, 2-5% conversion
- **Best Timing:** Week 1, Day 2-3 (after case study published)
- **Files:** `content/video/longform/pass1-content-cut.md`, `pass2-story-structure.md`

### 3. Short-Form Video Set (10 videos)
- **Purpose:** Social proof, shareability, multi-platform distribution
- **Audience:** LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube Shorts audiences
- **Expected Outcome:** 2,000-5,000 total views, 8-12% engagement rate, 5-8% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 1-2, staggered release (2-3 per week)
- **File:** `short-form-video-plan.md`

## B. RECOMMENDED ASSETS

### 4. Single-Image Post: Near Miss Reduction (STAT-DRIVEN)
- **Purpose:** Safety angle, high engagement, shareability
- **Audience:** Safety-conscious pharmacists, decision-makers
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,500-2,500 impressions, 20-25% engagement rate, 15-20% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 1, Day 3 (after case study)
- **Rationale:** Safety content performs exceptionally (24.4% engagement on near misses carousel)
- **Content:** "97% fewer near misses: 5-6/month → 2/year"

### 5. Single-Image Post: 85% Time Free (SERVICES ANGLE)
- **Purpose:** Services focus, time savings proof
- **Audience:** Service-focused pharmacy owners
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,200-2,000 impressions, 15-20% engagement, 10-15% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 1, Day 5
- **Rationale:** Services content resonates with growth-focused owners
- **Content:** "85% of my time, pretty much free" with services list

### 6. Carousel: Before/After Transformation (5-6 SLIDES)
- **Purpose:** Visual storytelling, scrollable engagement
- **Audience:** Visual learners, carousel-engaged audience
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,500-2,500 impressions, 18-22% engagement, 12-18% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 2, Day 1
- **Rationale:** Carousels perform well (48.4% engagement on Trustpilot carousel)
- **Structure:**
  1. Hook: "97% fewer near misses"
  2. Before: Chaos, 5-6 near misses/month
  3. After: Calm, 2 near misses/year
  4. Time: 85% freed for services
  5. Staff: Empowered, meaningful work
  6. CTA: Read full case study

### 7. Quote-Led Post: "I Have Zero Worries"
- **Purpose:** Emotional connection, shareability
- **Audience:** Stressed pharmacists, burnout prevention
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,000-1,800 impressions, 12-18% engagement, 8-12% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 2, Day 3
- **Rationale:** Emotional testimonials perform well
- **Content:** Large quote card with "I have zero worries" + context

### 8. Data-Led Post: Near Miss Statistics
- **Purpose:** Quantified proof, credibility
- **Audience:** Data-driven decision-makers
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,200-2,000 impressions, 20-25% engagement, 15-20% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 2, Day 5
- **Rationale:** Stat-driven content performs exceptionally (24.4% engagement)
- **Content:** Infographic showing 5-6/month → 2/year with visual comparison

### 9. Behind-the-Scenes Post: 80-Year-Old Using Titan
- **Purpose:** Objection handling, generational adoption
- **Audience:** Older pharmacists, tech-skeptical audience
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,000-1,500 impressions, 15-20% engagement, 10-15% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 3, Day 1
- **Rationale:** Unique angle, breaks common objection
- **Content:** Photo/video of Safey using Titan (if available) or quote card

### 10. Service-Angle Post: Expanding Services
- **Purpose:** Service growth narrative, revenue focus
- **Audience:** Service-focused owners, growth-minded
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,200-2,000 impressions, 12-18% engagement, 8-12% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 3, Day 3
- **Rationale:** Services content drives engagement
- **Content:** List of services (travel, weight loss, earwax, Botox) with quote

### 11. Patient Experience Post: Zero Lost Prescriptions
- **Purpose:** Patient-facing benefits, collection efficiency
- **Audience:** Patient-focused owners, quality-focused
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,000-1,800 impressions, 10-15% engagement, 6-10% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 3, Day 5
- **Rationale:** Patient experience resonates
- **Content:** "Never lost a prescription in a year" with barcode scanning visual

### 12. Cost Savings Post: Paperless Savings
- **Purpose:** ROI angle, material cost savings
- **Audience:** Cost-conscious owners, ROI-focused
- **Expected Outcome:** 1,000-1,500 impressions, 12-18% engagement, 8-12% CTR
- **Best Timing:** Week 4, Day 1
- **Rationale:** Cost savings add to ROI narrative

## E. ENGAGEMENT RATIONALE

### Driving Engagement:
- **Near miss reduction** (unique safety angle, concrete numbers)
- **85% time free** (clear benefit, quantifiable)
- **Zero worries** (emotional resonance, stress relief)
- **80-year-old using Titan** (objection handling, relatable)
- **Competitive advice humor** (shareability, memorable)

### Pushing Click-Through:
- Case study link in every post
- Strong CTAs ("Read full case study", "Book a demo")
- Stat-driven posts (high CTR historically)
- Carousel format (scrollable engagement)
- Video end screens with links

### Creating FOMO:
- Near miss reduction (safety concern, competitive advantage)
- Competitive area dynamics ("they'll go down the road")
- Service expansion (revenue opportunity)
- Time freedom (lifestyle benefit)
- Staff empowerment (retention advantage)

### Creating Pride (Existing Customers):
- Near miss reduction validates their choice
- Staff empowerment shows Titan's impact
- Patient experience improvements
- Community recognition (45 years + modern tech)
- Generational adoption success

- Have 2-3 backup assets ready
- Prepare response templates
- Monitor metrics daily, adjust quickly
- Repurpose top performers if needed
