# 2025-12-15 Safy's Chemist Case Study

**Case Study: How Safy's Chemist Cut Near Misses by 97% and Freed 85% of Pharmacist Time**

Safy's Chemist case study featuring Asif's transformation of a busy community pharmacy (14,500-15,000 items/month) that reduced near misses from 5-6 per month to just 2 in an entire year, freed 85% of pharmacist time for services, and empowered staff through Titan automation.

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** December 15, 2025  
**Product:** Titan PMR  
**Type:** Case Study  
**Subject:** Safy's Chemist, Melton Road (Asif)

## Key Metrics

- 97% reduction in near misses (5-6/month → 2/year)
- 85% of pharmacist time freed for services
- 14,500-15,000 items dispensed monthly
- 100% paperless workflow
- Zero lost prescriptions in a year
- 80-year-old superintendent using Titan successfully
