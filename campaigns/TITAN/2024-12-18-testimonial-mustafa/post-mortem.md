# Post-Mortem

**Campaign Name:** Mustafa — From Sceptical to Converted

**Product:** TITAN

**Objective:**
Showcase a real pharmacist testimonial that addresses skepticism toward Titan PMR and demonstrates measurable benefits through lived experience.

**Key Assets:**
- Short-form video testimonial (TikTok-style)

---

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Direct, authentic user voice:** Mustafa's honest testimonial resonated with skeptical audience
- **Addresses skepticism head-on:** Opening with "I've been very skeptical" creates immediate relatability
- **High CTR for video content:** 7.3% CTR indicates strong interest in video format
- **Clear benefits:** Reduced errors, eliminated bottlenecks, multi-location flexibility clearly communicated

### What Didn't Work

- **Watch time limited:** Average 10 seconds suggests video may be too short or not engaging enough
- **Minimal conversation in comments:** Only 1 comment indicates low engagement depth
- **Limited reach:** 958 impressions is moderate for testimonial content

### Audience Insights

- **Curiosity over engagement:** High CTR but low watch time suggests audience clicks but doesn't watch fully
- **Skeptical audience:** Content successfully reached skeptical audience but didn't drive deep engagement
- **Social proof value:** Qualitative value as social proof despite limited quantitative metrics

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Add text overlays:** Include "Before / After Titan" text overlays to enhance visual storytelling
- **Create carousel quoting transcript:** Repurpose testimonial into carousel format with key quotes
- **Run multi-platform testing:** Test on Shorts/TikTok/Reels to compare performance
- **Deeper CTA or follow-up:** Add link to follow-up series or longer-form content

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Authentic user voice, direct skepticism address, clear benefits
- **Add:** Text overlays, longer format option, compilation series link
- **Test:** Longer video format, carousel format, multi-platform distribution

### Process Improvements

- **Testimonial series:** Develop "I Didn't Believe It Either Until…" compilation series
- **Video length testing:** Test optimal length for testimonial videos (current 10 seconds may be too short)
- **Engagement tactics:** Include questions or prompts that encourage comments and discussion

---

## Next Steps

- Repurpose into future compilation — "I Didn't Believe It Either Until…"
- Create carousel format with transcript quotes
- Test longer video format (30-60 seconds) for deeper storytelling
- Develop multi-platform distribution strategy (Shorts/TikTok/Reels)
