# Mustafa — From Sceptical to Converted

Short-form testimonial campaign featuring Mustafa, a Titan PMR user who overcame early skepticism and now advocates for Titan based on reduced errors, remote working, and improved workflow efficiency.

## Purpose

Address market skepticism directly using authentic user voice.

## Primary Asset

- Short-form video testimonial (TikTok-style)

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post

## Outcome

Moderate reach but strong qualitative value as social proof. CTR indicates audience curiosity, though limited watch duration shows potential for deeper storytelling.

## Strategic Note

Repurpose into a future compilation — "I Didn't Believe It Either Until…"
