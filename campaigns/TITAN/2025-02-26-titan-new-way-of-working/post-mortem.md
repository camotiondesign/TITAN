# Post-Mortem

## Campaign Summary

**Campaign Name:** Titan New Way of Working  
**Date Range:** 2025-02-26  
**Product:** TITAN  
**Campaign Type:** Brand Positioning

**Objective:**
Position TITAN as a new way of working, not just another PMR, using pop culture visual reference (Space Odyssey) to communicate transformation.

- Single image (Space Odyssey monolith visual reference)

## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Pop culture reference worked:** Space Odyssey monolith visual reference effectively communicated transformation and innovation positioning
- **Good engagement:** 9.3% engagement rate and 7.1% CTR indicate strong interest in brand positioning content
- **Strong comments:** 9 comments from industry professionals validated brand positioning and "TITAN PMR way" messaging
- **Brand positioning validated:** Comments from Balal Anwar ("TITAN PMR way") and Meditech UK validated innovation positioning
- **"New way of working" messaging:** Content effectively positioned TITAN as transformative, not just another PMR
- **Contrast messaging:** Before/after contrast effectively communicated transformation
- **Follower growth:** 4 followers gained indicates content attracted new audience
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited discussion:** Only 9 comments despite 218 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark broader discussion
- **Could add quantified benefits:** Missing specific KPIs or measurable outcomes from "new way of working"

### Audience Insights

- **Pop culture references resonate:** Space Odyssey visual reference resonated with audience
- **Brand positioning works:** Content positioning TITAN as "new way of working" resonated strongly
- **Industry validation:** Comments from industry professionals (Balal Anwar, Meditech UK) validated brand positioning
- **"TITAN PMR way" messaging:** Comments reinforced "TITAN PMR way" brand positioning
- **Transformation narrative effective:** Content contrasting legacy PMR limitations with TITAN's modern approach resonated

- **Create brand positioning series:** Build series of brand positioning content using different visual references

- **Keep:** Pop culture visual references, brand positioning messaging, contrast messaging, "new way of working" narrative
