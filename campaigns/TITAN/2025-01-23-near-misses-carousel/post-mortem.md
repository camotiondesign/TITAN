## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Quantified safety proof (500 near misses):** Clear, specific statistic captured attention and built trust
- **Stat-driven angle:** Quantified proof points consistently high-performing and trust-building for pharmacists
- **Clear CTA:** Direct call-to-action drove exceptional click-through (22.9% CTR)
- **Safety-focused messaging:** Content addressed core pharmacist concern about patient safety
- **Carousel format:** Multi-slide format effectively communicated multiple safety benefits
- **Exceptional performance:** 24.4% engagement rate and 22.9% CTR demonstrate strong audience interest

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 367 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **Low reactions relative to clicks:** 17 reactions vs 344 clicks suggests content was informative but not emotionally engaging
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion

### Audience Insights

- **Safety content resonates:** Exceptional engagement (24.4%) indicates strong demand for safety-focused content
- **Quantified proof builds trust:** Stat-driven angle consistently high-performing and trust-building for pharmacists
- **Click-through indicates value:** High CTR (22.9%) suggests audience found content valuable and worth exploring
- **Safety is core concern:** Content addressing patient safety resonated strongly with pharmacy professionals
- **Stat-driven content performs:** Quantified proof points more effective than general claims
