## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Wrapped format:** The Spotify-style wrapped format resonated strongly with the audience
- **Metrics-driven content:** Showcasing concrete achievements (900+ sites, 96M prescriptions, 150+ updates, 200+ reviews) provided clear value
- **Year-end timing:** Posting at the end of 2024 capitalized on reflection and celebration moments
- **Collaborative ideation:** Wahid's idea, executed by Cam, showed the value of team input
- **Visual carousel format:** The 7-slide carousel format allowed for digestible, scrollable content

### What Didn't Work

- N/A - Post performed exceptionally well for its time

### Audience Insights

- **High engagement:** 65.1% engagement rate suggests the wrapped format and metrics resonated strongly
- **Strong click intent:** 63.2% CTR indicates audience wanted to learn more or visit the website
- **Positive sentiment:** Comments were congratulatory and supportive (Nas Khaliq, Balal Anwar, Mostafa Damghani, AlphaRize)
- **Brand recognition:** Comments from known partners and customers showed strong brand relationships

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Repeat wrapped format:** Consider annual wrapped campaigns as a consistent year-end tradition
- **Expand metrics:** Include more diverse metrics (customer satisfaction, team growth, feature launches)
- **Multi-platform:** Consider adapting wrapped format for TikTok and YouTube
- **Earlier planning:** Plan wrapped content earlier to allow for more comprehensive data collection

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Wrapped format, metrics showcase, year-end timing, visual carousel structure
- **Consider:** Adding video elements, expanding to more slides if metrics warrant, including customer quotes

### Process Improvements

- **Collaborative ideation:** Continue leveraging team ideas (like Wahid's suggestion) for content concepts
- **Performance tracking:** This post established baseline metrics for future comparison
- **Documentation:** Strong documentation of this campaign helps replicate success

---

## Next Steps

- Use this campaign as a benchmark for future LinkedIn carousel performance
- Plan 2025 wrapped campaign earlier in the year
- Consider expanding wrapped format to other platforms
- Maintain metrics-driven content approach for brand momentum posts
