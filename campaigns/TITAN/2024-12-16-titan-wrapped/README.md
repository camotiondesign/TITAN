# Titan 2024 Wrapped

A year-end brand momentum campaign summarising Titan's growth and impact across 2024.

Inspired by the Spotify Wrapped trend, delivered as a LinkedIn carousel.

## Core Proof Points

- 900+ sites live
- 96M prescriptions processed
- 150+ updates shipped
- 200+ 5-star TrustPilot reviews

## Outcome

High click-through, useful momentum narrative for sales, PR, and future marketing assets.

---

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** December 16, 2024  
**Product:** Titan PMR  
**Type:** Brand Momentum  
**Purpose:** Brand momentum, annual impact summary, social proof, and customer appreciation

## Performance Highlights

- Impressions: 1,811
- Reach: 924
- Engagement Rate: 65.1%
- Click-Through Rate: 63.2%
- Total Engagements: 1,179
