# 2025-07-01 Puri Pharmacy Case Study

**Case Study: How Puri Pharmacy Doubled Services and Went Fully Digital**

Puri Pharmacy case study featuring Rahul Puri's transformation of a two-branch operation (Southall and Hillingdon) handling 18,000+ items/month, going fully paperless, doubling appointment capacity, and scaling services without chaos.

## Campaign Overview

**Published:** July 1, 2025  
**Product:** Titan PMR  
**Type:** Case Study  
**Subject:** Puri Pharmacy, Southall and Hillingdon (Rahul Puri)

## Key Metrics

- 20+ hours per week freed up per pharmacist
- 18,000+ items per month across two branches
- 100% paperless workflow
- Pharmacy First capacity doubled
- Service expansion: phlebotomy, earwax, contraception, weight loss, cannabis consultations
