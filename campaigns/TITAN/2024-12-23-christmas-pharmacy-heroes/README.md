# Christmas — Pharmacy Heroes Appreciation

A seasonal brand morale message thanking pharmacy professionals for their dedication in 2024. This post reinforces Titan's positioning as a brand aligned with the pharmacy community's challenges and contributions.

## Primary Asset

- Single image post featuring Christmas message and pharmacy profession labels

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post

## Outcome

Good engagement for non-product content, positive sentiment, and community acknowledgment. Useful as part of annual morale content.

## Strategic Note

Establish recurring cadence for profession appreciation dates (Christmas, Pharmacy Team Appreciation Day, New Year message, etc.).
