## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Human and community-centred tone:** Messaging resonated with pharmacy professionals
- **Seasonal timing increasing goodwill:** Posted before holiday break maximized appreciation impact
- **Light-hearted visual reinforces message:** Snowman graphic with pharmacy role labels created engaging visual
- **Brand alignment:** Reinforced Titan's positioning as aligned with pharmacy community

### What Didn't Work

- **Click-through predictable low for non-product content:** 3.7% CTR expected for appreciation messaging
- **Message not tied to 2025 roadmap or value link:** No forward-looking connection or product tie-in
- **Limited conversation:** Only 2 comments suggests message was consumed but not discussed

### Audience Insights

- **Appreciation over engagement:** Audience sees this as appreciation, not CTA
- **Community value:** Reposts (4) and positive reactions indicate community appreciation
- **Seasonal relevance:** Timing before holiday break maximized impact

---

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Add subtle forward tease:** Include light reference to 2025 roadmap or upcoming features
- **Include link to "Year Wrapped":** Add link to year-end content for ecosystem continuity
- **Test video "Thank you message":** Create video message from team for more personal touch
- **Establish recurring cadence:** Plan for Pharmacy Team Appreciation Day, New Year message, etc.

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Human-centred tone, seasonal timing, community acknowledgment, light-hearted visuals
- **Add:** Subtle forward-looking elements, links to related content, video format option
- **Test:** Video format, carousel with multiple appreciation messages, interactive elements

### Process Improvements

- **Appreciation calendar:** Develop calendar of profession appreciation dates throughout the year
- **Template development:** Create template for seasonal appreciation messages
- **Ecosystem linking:** Connect appreciation messages to product content or year-end summaries
