## Learnings

### What Worked

- **Government endorsement:** Eluned Morgan MS quote added credibility and demonstrated NHS alignment
- **Bilingual messaging:** English and Welsh slides demonstrated cultural sensitivity and local relevance
- **Milestone positioning:** Content successfully positioned EPS rollout as major achievement
- **Strong engagement:** 23.2% engagement rate and 21.6% CTR demonstrate high audience interest
- **Historical context:** Reference to November 2023 first deployment added depth and credibility
- **Reposts indicate value:** 4 reposts suggest content was valuable for sharing with peers
- **NHS alignment messaging:** Content demonstrated NHS alignment and innovation leadership

### What Didn't Work

- **Limited comments:** Only 1 comment despite 287 engagements suggests content was consumed but didn't spark discussion
- **No discussion prompts:** Missing questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Welsh engagement unclear:** Unable to determine if Welsh slide increased engagement from Welsh audience

### Audience Insights

- **Milestone announcements resonate:** Strong engagement (23.2%) indicates high interest in milestone achievements
- **Government endorsement works:** Eluned Morgan MS quote added credibility and demonstrated NHS alignment
- **Click-through indicates value:** High CTR (21.6%) suggests audience found content valuable and worth exploring
- **Bilingual content demonstrates sensitivity:** English and Welsh slides showed cultural awareness
- **Historical context adds depth:** Reference to first deployment in November 2023 added credibility

## Recommendations

### For Future Similar Campaigns

- **Continue government endorsement:** Government quotes consistently add credibility and demonstrate NHS alignment
- **Add discussion prompts:** Include questions or prompts to encourage comments and discussion
- **Test bilingual variants:** Experiment with different bilingual approaches or language-specific posts
- **Expand to other formats:** Repurpose carousel content into blog posts, videos, or other formats
- **Build milestone library:** Create library of milestone announcements for future reference

### Content Adjustments

- **Keep:** Government endorsement, bilingual messaging, milestone positioning, historical context
- **Add:** Discussion prompts, results follow-up, promotion strategy, results carousel
- **Test:** Different bilingual approaches, language-specific posts, milestone celebration formats

### Process Improvements

- **Milestone tracking:** Develop process for tracking and celebrating milestones
- **Government engagement:** Develop process for securing and leveraging government endorsements
- **Discussion strategy:** Develop prompts and questions to encourage engagement and comments
- **Bilingual content workflow:** Develop process for creating bilingual content
