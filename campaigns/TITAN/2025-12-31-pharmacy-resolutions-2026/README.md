# Pharmacy Resolutions 2026

**Date:** 2025-12-31  
**Format:** Carousel (7 slides)  
**Purpose:** New Year pharmacy resolutions checklist with transformation focus, motivating pharmacies to set goals for 2026.

## Overview

A motivational carousel presenting key pharmacy resolutions for 2026. Focuses on transformation, efficiency, and growth. Encourages audience to share their own resolutions.

- **Slide 1:** Title slide
- **Slide 2:** Scale safely

- **Slide 4:** Expand services

- Scale safely without chaos
- Free pharmacist time for services
- Expand services revenue
- Reduce near misses by 90%+

- Encourages community to share resolutions
