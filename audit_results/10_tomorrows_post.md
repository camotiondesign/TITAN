# Phase 10: Tomorrow's Post Creation

## Narrative Gap Identified

**Missing element**: The moment of realization - when a pharmacy owner recognizes their PMR is the bottleneck, not their team or their processes. Current content shows "before" and "after" but skips the turning point.

**Why this matters**: The recognition moment is where change begins. Showing this moment helps other pharmacy owners recognize their own situation.

## Underused Pain Point

**Problem**: "The scale ceiling" - pharmacies hit growth limits imposed by their PMR, but they often blame themselves, their team, or market conditions. The PMR as the limiting factor is mentioned but not fully explored.

**Why underused**: Most posts focus on outcomes (more items, less stress) but not on the recognition that the system was the problem all along.
