# Proof Pillar

**Case studies, testimonials, results, and social proof**

---

## What Belongs Here

- Pharmacy case studies
- Customer testimonials
- Results and metrics
- Transformation stories
- Before/After comparisons
- Success stories
- Trustpilot reviews and social proof

---

## Performance Targets

Based on analytics (56 posts analyzed):
- **Avg Engagement Rate:** 20.0%
- **Avg CTR:** 18.8%
- **Avg Impressions:** 1,791
- **Target Mix:** 25-30% of total content (currently 44% — reduce volume, improve quality)

---

## Content Format Recommendations

- **Carousels** — Transformation stories, case study highlights (60%+ engagement)
- **Videos** — Longform case studies, testimonial videos
- **Blogs** — Full case studies (see CASE_STUDY_PLAYBOOK.md)
- **Single Images** — Quote graphics, stat posts

---

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Quality over quantity** — Focus on strong case studies, not volume
2. **Transformation arc** — Clear before/after contrast
3. **Specific metrics** — Use numbers, not vague claims
4. **Emotional quotes** — Real quotes from real pharmacists
5. **Longer captions** — 151+ words perform 2.5x better
