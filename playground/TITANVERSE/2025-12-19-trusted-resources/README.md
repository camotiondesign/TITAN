# Trusted Resources

Single image campaign highlighting closed system learning from trusted UK clinical resources - not Google, but NICE and the Green Book. Safety through quality sources.

## Primary Asset

- Single image (trusted resources)

## Supporting Assets

- LinkedIn post

## Strategic Note

Single image positions closed system and trusted UK clinical resources as safety foundation. Emphasizes learning from NICE and Green Book, not generic web sources. Addresses trust concern subtly by highlighting rigorous clinical foundation. Creates curiosity about safety and quality sources. Mid-week trust/safety moment addressing "how can we trust it" question after operational (Post 1) and experience (Post 2) narratives.
