# How Puri Pharmacy Doubled Services and Went Fully Digital

Puri Pharmacy is a two-branch, family-run pharmacy serving the communities of Southall and Hillingdon. Together, the team dispenses approximately 18,000 items per month while delivering a wide range of NHS and private services. Since switching to Titan in early 2025, both branches have gone fully paperless, doubled appointment capacity, and adopted a digital-first model that supports high-volume dispensing and service-led growth without the chaos.

## How they did it

### 1. Led from the Front, Digitised at the Core

Rahul Puri isn't just the superintendent pharmacist at Puri Pharmacy. He's part of a new generation of pharmacy leaders who blend clinical care, digital innovation and creative communication. When he's not running services, he's on TikTok reaching thousands with videos like "What does your mucus say about you?"

### 2. Replacing Frantic Workflows with Structure and Calm

With Titan in place, both pharmacies are now fully paperless. Every team member sees the same screen, prescriptions are tracked at every stage, and stock is picked without guesswork or disruption.

> "Everyone can see where the prescription is. No one is frantically looking for paper. We've become a paper free pharmacy."
>
> "Now we're fully embracing the digital space in pharmacy. We're busy, but we're never chaotic."

That visibility has created a calmer, more structured environment. Staff are more confident, patients are better served, and the daily rush doesn't feel like firefighting anymore.

### 3. Freeing Up Time to Deliver More Care

Titan Repeat Flow, Titan AI, and integrated SMS have transformed the dispensary into a system that runs smoothly in the background. Pharmacists are no longer stuck checking baskets. That time has been redirected into services.

> "Since implementing Titan in January 2025, it's freed up at least 20 hours a week for a pharmacist in the pharmacy."
>
> "Now we're not having to do checking or archaic tasks we used to do with ProScript. We can offer double the amount of appointments."

Patients now receive prescriptions faster, with automated text alerts, shelf tracking and QR code collection.

> "Patients are collecting their medicines far earlier, and we're actually looking for more work to do."

With the pressure off, Rahul and the team have expanded far beyond traditional pharmacy services. The business now delivers weight loss clinics, phlebotomy, private medical cannabis consultations, contraception and more.

> "I don't need to be worried about someone knocking on my door during a service. The system allows for calm and no chaos."

The impact is visible in both branches, not just in service volume, but in how the pharmacy is perceived.

> "We're changing our business model from being just a dispensary to a community health hub."
