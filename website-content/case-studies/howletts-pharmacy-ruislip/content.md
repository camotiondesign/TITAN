Howletts Pharmacy in Ruislip is a community pharmacy built on calm, clarity and care. Under superintendent and owner Pritee, the team dispenses around 6,500 items per month, runs a busy post office, and delivers both NHS and private services. Since switching to Titan, the pharmacy has gone fully digital, built a safer, calmer workflow, and created space for staff to upskill and patients to receive more time and attention.

## The Challenge

Before Titan, Howletts Pharmacy was running on paper-heavy, manual systems that consumed staff time and kept Pritee tied to the bench. End-of-month claims could take days, accuracy checks piled up on the workbench, and staff were bogged down in repetitive processes.

The setup limited growth, delayed services, and created unnecessary stress for both pharmacists and dispensers. For Pritee, the aim was to redesign the business into a digital-first, service-led pharmacy where the team could work calmly, efficiently, and with confidence.
