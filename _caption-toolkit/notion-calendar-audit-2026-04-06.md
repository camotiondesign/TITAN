1. **Max 2 posts per day** (one Titan, one Titanverse if publishing both brands same day)
2. **No back-to-back videos** — space video posts by at least 3 days per brand
3. **Case study posts need a gap** — don't run two case study posts in the same week (dilutes each one)
4. **Seasonal posts are fixed** — Eid Al Adha (May 27) is anchored, plan around it
5. **Fridays work for cultural/fun content** — memes, photo dumps, relatable posts
6. **Tuesdays and Wednesdays** are strongest for educational and case study posts
7. **Don't schedule before you have the design** — if Cam hasn't briefed the design, leave as "Not started" not "Concept for Review"
