## Design Brief (from Notion)

OBSERVATION POST — NO CUSTOMER QUOTE, NO PRODUCT MENTION IN GRAPHIC. Single image, editorial feel. Headline text: Five tools. Five logins. Five points of failure. White text on dark background. No Titanverse UI, no product screenshots. This is an observation post that names the pain without pitching the solution. Titanverse logo subtle bottom corner only. Design should feel like an industry editorial, not a software vendor ad. Positioning: Fragmentation Problem (TV Theme 2). Makes status quo visible. Audience connects the dots themselves.
