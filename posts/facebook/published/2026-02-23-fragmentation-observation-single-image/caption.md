How many subscriptions do you pay for to run one consultation?

Because more than one is already too many.

A booking platform.
A consultation tool.
A separate prescribing system.
Paper risk assessments.
Then re-entering everything back into the PMR.

Five. Six. Seven logins.
More tabs.
More chances for something to slip through.

And pharmacists wonder why scaling services feels impossible.

It is not a motivation problem.
It is not a skills problem.
It is not even a time problem.

It is an infrastructure problem.

The pharmacy industry built incredible clinicians
and then handed them a patchwork of tools that were never designed to work together.

Until that changes,
“just do more services” will keep sounding simple
and feeling impossible.

That is exactly what Titanverse was built to fix.
One platform for bookings, consultations, prescribing, and compliance.
