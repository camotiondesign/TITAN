# TPMR_TitanUp2026 – Alt Text

## Design Brief

9-page carousel announcing TitanUp 2026, a high-impact event for TITAN pharmacy customers on June 7th 2026 in Birmingham. Uses a mystery-reveal narrative structure with alternating light and dark slides.

## Alt Text

**Page 1:** White background. Large bold black text: "WE'VE BEEN KEEPING SOMETHING QUIET." TITAN logo centred at the bottom.

**Page 2:** White background. Large bold black text: "BUT SOME OF YOU ALREADY KNOW."

**Page 3:** Dark background. A card element containing "For TITAN customers only" text above a QR code with 👀 emoji eyes, and "SCAN ME" below. Beneath the card, text reads: "WHO SAW THIS LAST WEEK?" — referencing the QR code post from the day before.

**Page 4:** White background. Bold/medium black text: "IF YOU SCANNED IT... You already know what's coming."

**Page 5:** White background. Bold black text: "FOR EVERYONE ELSE..."

**Page 6:** White background. Three drum emojis centred: 🥁🥁🥁 — a drumroll teaser beat.

**Page 7:** Dark background. An orange right-pointing arrow graphic, with bold green and white text: "TITAN UP 26 IS HERE."

**Page 8:** Dark background. Event details overlaid on a wide crowd photo from a live event: "📍 BIRMINGHAM / 📅 JUNE 7TH 2026 / Your Pharmacy Business. Supercharged." Crowd of pharmacy professionals visible, TITAN-branded stage in background.

**Page 9:** Dark background with a glowing wireframe globe graphic. Full CTA text: "TITAN UP 26 · BIRMINGHAM · JUNE 7TH 2026 · LIMITED CAPACITY · TITANUP.CLUB"
