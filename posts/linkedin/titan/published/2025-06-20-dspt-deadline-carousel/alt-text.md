## Slide 1 — Deadline warning

Slide branded with Community Pharmacy England stating that nearly half of pharmacies have not submitted their Data Security and Protection Toolkit, highlighting a submission deadline of 30 June 2025 and warning that failure results in an inadequate governance rating under NHS contractual requirements.

---

## Slide 2 — What is DSPT?

Slide explaining that the Data Security and Protection Toolkit is a mandatory NHS requirement confirming pharmacies meet information governance and data protection standards.

---

## Slide 3 — Why DSPT matters

Slide outlining why submission is important, noting it is required for NHS funding and for delivering new pharmacy services, and warning that missing the deadline results in an inadequate governance rating.

---

## Slide 4 — Who must submit

Slide explaining that all community pharmacies in England must submit the toolkit, even if they completed it the previous year, as annual resubmission is required.

---

## Slide 5 — Where to submit

Slide showing where pharmacies must complete submission online at dsptoolkit.nhs.uk, including a screenshot of the NHS Data Security and Protection Toolkit login page.
