**Rahul Puri:**  
Two years ago, we were frantically using paper diaries, post-it notes, prescriptions everywhere — scribbling everywhere. Now everything is clearly visible on each screen, from the inbox when you receive a prescription all the way to the final handout. Everyone can see where the prescription is. No one is frantically looking for paper. We've become a paper-free pharmacy.
