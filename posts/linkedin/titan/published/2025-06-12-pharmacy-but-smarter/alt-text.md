## Alt text

Flowchart illustrating Titan's prescription workflow. A prescription arrives automatically from the NHS Spine, then AI performs safety, dose, and interaction checks. Prescriptions marked safe move to dispensing, while those flagged not safe are reviewed by a pharmacist before proceeding. The dispenser prepares the prescription, the patient is notified via SMS with a QR code, and finally the patient collects their medication, completing the process.
