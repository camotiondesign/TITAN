## Slide 2 — What is clawback?

Slide titled "WHAT IS CLAWBACK?" in large blue and black text. Body copy explains that the CPCF sets a target medicines margin for the sector, and if total margin goes above that target, reimbursement prices are adjusted to recover the difference. An illustrated pharmacist points at a chart comparing "Allowed Profit" with a taller bar labelled "Excess". TITAN logo bottom left.

## Slide 3 — Here is the catch

Slide titled "HERE IS THE CATCH". Text explains that clawback is based on the whole industry, not individual pharmacies, and that if big chains make excess profit, other pharmacies still pay. Illustration shows a tall figure labelled "BIG CHAIN" beside a much smaller pharmacist and two bar columns, reinforcing the imbalance. TITAN logo bottom left.
