# TPMR_PrabTestimonial – Alt Text

## Design Brief

Branded testimonial card featuring Prab, owner of Priory Pharmacy, sharing how TITAN PMR transformed his working life.

## Alt Text

Professional branded testimonial card. White/light background. Large pull-quote in bold blue text: "If it wasn't for Titan, I'd have left pharmacy a long time ago." Below the quote: a headshot of a smiling man wearing glasses and a dark navy turban, dressed in a blue zip-up jumper. Attribution reads: "Prab, Pharmacy Owner / Priory Pharmacy." TITAN logo in the bottom-right corner.
