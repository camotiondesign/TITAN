## Design Brief (from Notion)

Clip focus: Sajid's quote about pharmacists getting to go home on time, not working weekends, enjoying family life. The emotional core is the human impact, not the product.

"It's pharmacists just saying like, I get to go home on time. I don't have to work weekends. I don't need to come in early. These are the stories what we're all looking at because, at the end of the day, business is business, but at the end of the day, you wanna enjoy your family life as well."

If the full clip is too long for LinkedIn optimal (90-120 seconds), trim to the strongest 60 seconds focusing on the 'go home on time' section.
