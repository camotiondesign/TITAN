"We were wasting time chasing repeats before Titan. Now that time goes into services and revenue."

That's Rahul at Puri Pharmacy.

Here's what repeat management looks like without a system:

- Confirm a date with the patient
- Calculate when the order needs to go to the surgery
- File it away and remember to pull it in time
- Fax it or type out an email
- Wait for the prescription to come back
- Manually reconcile what the surgery sent
- Chase anything that's missing

Every patient. Every time.

Now after the first entry, Titan handles the whole thing automatically. Orders go out on time. Prescriptions get reconciled before the pharmacist sees them. Missing items get flagged without anyone remembering to check.

The team only touches it when there's a genuine clinical reason to.

"If your repeats rely on memory and chasing, you don't have a system. You have a bottleneck."

Same team. Different system.
