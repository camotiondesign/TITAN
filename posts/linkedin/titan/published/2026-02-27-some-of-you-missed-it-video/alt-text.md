# TPMR_SomeOfYouMissedIt – Alt Text

## Design Brief

Short teaser video re-surfacing the TitanUp QR code for anyone who missed it, set against footage from a previous TITAN UP live event.

## Alt Text

Short teaser video combining two visual elements in a split composition: the left/foreground shows the "For TITAN customers only" QR code graphic with "SCAN ME 👀" text; the right/background shows footage from a previous live TITAN UP event — a diverse, enthusiastic audience applauding in a conference venue, with "TITAN UP — Innovation and Beyond." branding visible on the stage backdrop.
