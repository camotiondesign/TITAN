# TitanUp QR Code Loop - Video – Alt Text

Promotional graphic for TITAN customers featuring a QR code and the text "For TITAN customers only" and "SCAN ME". Around the code, clips from a previous Titan Up event rotate in a radial layout, showing audience moments, on-stage branding, networking scenes and event materials. A pair of cartoon eyes appears beside the QR code, with dark background gradients and green accents.
