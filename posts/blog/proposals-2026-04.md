## Notes on Blog Format for titanpmr.com / titanverse.co.uk

- **Length:** 800–1,200 words. Not longer. The audience is busy pharmacists, not content marketers.
- **Headings:** Use clear H2 headings. Pharmacists scan, they don't read linearly.
- **One real example per blog.** Always name the pharmacy (with permission) or reference real data.
- **No fluff intro.** Start with the stat or the problem. Not "As the healthcare landscape evolves..."
- **CTA:** One CTA, at the end. Not multiple. Not mid-article.
- **Internal links:** Link to at least one case study and one product page per blog.
- **SEO note:** Include the target keyword (e.g., "Pharmacy First compliance," "hub and spoke dispensing") in the H1 and once in the first paragraph. Do not stuff keywords.
