# Best Hooks Catalog

## How We Define "Best Hooks"

**Primary Metrics (Weighted):**
- **Engagement Rate (40%)**: Shows how well hook stops scroll and drives interaction
- **CTR (30%)**: Shows intent to learn more (especially for single images)
- **Impressions (20%)**: Shows reach/algorithmic favor
- **Activational Metrics (10%)**: Followers gained, page views, comments (intent-driven actions)

**Format Criteria:**
- **First 2 lines are most critical** (LinkedIn preview)
- **Format**: 2 short punchy lines OR one decent-width sentence that wraps to second line
- Must work within LinkedIn's character limit for preview (first ~150 characters)

## Hook Type Classifications

1. **POV Statement**: "POV: You start a consultation." - First-person perspective
2. **Reframing Statement**: "Your Pharmacy Isn't Struggling. Your PMR Is." - Shifts blame/frame
3. **Question Hook**: "Still building templates by hand?" - Creates "yes" moment
4. **Transformation Statement**: "Pharmacy has transformed..." - Industry evolution
5. **Contrast Statement**: "Two pharmacies. Same building." - Visual/ideological contrast
6. **Revenue Opportunity**: "The money is in services." - Financial benefit
7. **Evolution Narrative**: "Titan freed you... Now it's time..." - Clear progression
8. **List Format**: "3 Automation Tools..." - Educational/curiosity-driven
9. **Celebration/Metrics**: "‚ú® TITAN 2024 Wrapped ‚ú®" - Positive emotion
10. **Visual Statement**: "This is what a modern pharmacy looks like." - Promises visual

## Format Patterns

### 2 Short Punchy Lines (Most Common)
- "Your Pharmacy Isn't Struggling." / "Your PMR Is."
- "Pharmacy is shifting fast." / "The money is in services."
- "Titan freed you from dispensing." / "Now it's time for services."
- "Two pharmacies. Same building." / "Very different future."

### One Sentence Wrapping (Also Effective)
- "This is what a modern pharmacy looks like." (wraps naturally)
- "Pharmacy has transformed in the last five years." (wraps naturally)
- "Still building consultation templates by hand?" (wraps naturally)
- "AI is allowing pharmacies to focus on growth & care" (wraps naturally)

---

## What Makes Hooks Work

1. **Immediate contrast/reframing** - Shifts perspective instantly
2. **Specific, not generic** - Concrete details over vague statements
3. **Addresses pain point or opportunity** - Speaks to real concerns
4. **Creates curiosity gap** - Makes reader want to know more
5. **Uses active voice** - Direct and engaging
6. **Short and scannable** - Works in LinkedIn preview
7. **First 2 lines are critical** - Must work within preview limit
